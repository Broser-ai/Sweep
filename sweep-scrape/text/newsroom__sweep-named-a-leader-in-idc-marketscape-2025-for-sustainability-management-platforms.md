Sweep Named a Leader in IDC MarketScape 2025 for Sustainability Management Platforms
Sweep has been recognized as a Leader in the 2025 IDC MarketScape for Worldwide Sustainability Management Platforms*, affirming its market position as a top-tier ESG solution provider.

This recognition highlights Sweep’s leadership in carbon accounting and as a full ESG data management platform, positioning it as a comprehensive sustainability solution, structured around three core pillars: Track, Disclose, Act.
Key highlights from the IDC report
Leading on data management
Sweep’s unified, scalable architecture simplifies data centralization, supported by:
– Smart AI-assisted data mapping
– Flexible integrations
– Automated validation and compliance
Built-in goal-setting & ROI modeling
Enables users to:
– Set and track sustainability targets
– Forecast outcomes and calculate ROI
– Drive sustainability as a business growth lever
Double materiality & IRO tools
– Sector-agnostic assessments aligned with frameworks like GRI and CSRD
– Intelligent automation to reduce manual data work and focus on what matters
Global supplier engagement
– Custom survey tools and emissions estimates
– Maturity-adapted version of the platform for suppliers to develop their sustainability practice
IDC, the US-headquartered global market intelligence leader, evaluated
21 vendors
that met strict inclusion criteria across capability, scale, and geographic reach.
Sweep’s continued innovation and impact-driven design make it a trusted partner for companies aligning sustainability goals with business performance.
