Sweep and CFGI announce strategic partnership to help enterprises navigate sustainability reporting with confidence
Category
News Announcement
Last updated
April 14, 2026
News announcement
This strategic partnership combines Sweep’s market-leading sustainability intelligence platform with CFGI’s finance, accounting, and ESG expertise, ensuring quality non-financial information for effective decision-making and regulatory compliance.
Together, Sweep and CFGI will help enterprises meet growing sustainability reporting requirements with audit-ready data and processes. The partnership brings together Sweep’s technology platform for sustainability data management with CFGI’s deep expertise in technical accounting, internal controls, regulatory compliance, and ESG program management. The result is a complete solution that ensures you can capture and manage your data to meet your regulatory requirements and support your strategic objectives.
As CFOs and finance leaders face mounting pressure to deliver on growing compliance demands, such as CSRD, IFRS S1/S2 and California SB 253/SB 261, organizations need more than guidance on simply what’s required. More than ever, they need technology and processes that: can handle data complexity; meet audit standards; scale with the growth in data volumes; and keep up with regulatory change. This partnership addresses each of these challenges.
Sweep’s enterprise-grade sustainability intelligence platform gives organizations the infrastructure to collect, manage, and report sustainability data with the granularity and traceability that auditors and regulators expect. Sweep supports comprehensive value chain emissions tracking, meets industry-standard reporting frameworks, and delivers the data quality and controls that finance teams require.
CFGI brings end-to-end sustainability capabilities, supporting clients across the full ESG lifecycle.  The company works alongside management to translate continuously-evolving ESG requirements into practical, scalable solutions. CFGI does this whilst building clients internal capabilities so that their combined work can continue to be taken forward. The outcome is sustainability information that is decision-useful, credible and audit ready while building robust foundations for long-term value creation.
Rachel Delacour, CEO and cofounder of Sweep said:
“Finance leaders are on the front line of sustainability reporting, and they need solutions built for their standards – audit trails, data integrity, and regulatory precision. CFGI understands the compliance and assurance requirements that CFOs and controllers are managing, and together we can give them both the strategic guidance and the technology platform to get it done. This partnership is particularly well-suited to the US and European markets, where organizations are balancing CSRD extraterritorial scope and California mandates all at once.”
Chris Docherty, Head of ESG Advisory at CFGI adds:
“Sustainability reporting is continuing to move from voluntary to being mandated by an increasingly complex regulatory landscape. Organizations need confidence that their data, processes and controls will stand up to regulatory and assurance scrutiny. Partnering with Sweep allows us to combine deep ESG, accounting and controls expertise with a robust, enterprise-grade technology platform. Together, we help teams move beyond interpretation of requirements to operationalise them – building audit-ready sustainability data and governance that supports compliance, decision-making and long-term business resilience.”
About Sweep
Sweep is the enterprise sustainability intelligence platform trusted by the world’s leading companies and financial institutions. Our SaaS solution helps organizsations meet complex reporting requirements, map environmental impact across their value chain, and strategically implement sustainability strategies that deliver business value.
Sweep delivers transparent, granular, and actionable data-backed insights. Our platform empowers decision-makers to identify regulatory and climate risks, engage their supply chain, design robust ESG transition plans, and build long-term business resilience.
From medium-sized companies to global giants like L’Oréal, Orange, and Crocs, our clients use Sweep to embed sustainability at the core of their operations and stay competitive in a rapidly evolving regulatory and economic landscape.
Sweep has been recogniszed by Verdantix, IDC, and MEDEF as a leader in sustainability data management.
Co-founded by Rachel Delacour, Yannick Chaze, and Raphael Gueller, we are B Corp certified and active members of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition, France Invest, and the International Emissions Trading Association.

About CFGI
CFGI is a leading international accounting and business advisory firm, supporting public and private organisations across all industries. CFGI offers a full suite of solutions for all critical matters facing the office of the CFO and investors, including technical and operational accounting, finance transformation, tax, risk advisory, audit readiness and ESG advisory. Comprised of former Big 4 professionals and with no audit practice, CFGI provides the responsivity and bespoke solutions of a boutique advisory with the top tier expertise and international scale of the world’s leading professional services firms.
CFGI’s ESG advisory practice focuses on providing practical support to organisations that delivers regulatory compliance and strategic value. We deliver projects in tandem with client teams in ways that make sense for that specific team and organisation.  We support clients across all aspects of their sustainability journey including regulatory mapping and compliance; report preparation, operating and governance models, process and control development, risk and materiality assessments, GHG accounting, transition planning, assurance readiness and platform implementations. Drawing on its broader finance and accounting capabilities, CFGI helps organisations embed sustainability into their businesses in a way that is scalable, audit
