​Join Sweep and Quantis for an exclusive roundtable lunch with 15 sustainability leaders from the cosmetics industry.
​We’ll discuss how leading brands are navigating economic and regulatory headwinds, while demonstrating that sustainability initiatives are a strategic asset for growth and resilience.
​During this lunch, we’ll cover:
​
Actionable strategies:
Learn how to translate sustainability data into tangible business growth within the US cosmetics market.
​
Real-world success stories:
See how a global beauty brand transformed sustainability initiatives into measurable business impact.
​
The role of technology:
discover how technology can streamline reporting (in line with frameworks such as CSRD, ISSB, GRI and SB 253), improve accountability, and result in business competitive advantage.
​
Understand why sustainability is smart business:
Looking at the ROI of sustainable practices and the cost of inaction in the US cosmetics industry.
