Presented for the first time during COP30 — and officially integrated into the UNFCCC Action Agenda alongside initiatives such as SBTi, PCAF, and the GHG Protocol
the Climate Contribution Framework is filling a gap by providing the first ever means of measuring global climate action at corporate level
.
Developed by Sweep, Mirova, I Care by Bearing Point and Winrock International, the Contribution Framework provides a clear, credible, and actionable way to measure how companies contribute to global net zero — moving the market from mere ambition to accountability.

to the global deployment of the Climate Contribution Framework
