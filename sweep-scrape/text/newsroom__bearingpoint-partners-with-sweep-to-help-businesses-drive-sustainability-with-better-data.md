BearingPoint Partners with Sweep to help businesses drive sustainability with better data
BearingPoint partners with Sweep, an AI-powered sustainability platform, to help businesses manage carbon & ESG data and meet CSRD requirements for smarter decisions.
Category
News Announcement
Last updated
January 07, 2026
News announcement
14 January 2025
BearingPoint is proud to announce its new partnership with Sweep, a leading, AI-powered sustainability data management platform recognized by industry analysts including IDC and Verdantix. This collaboration is designed to help businesses make smarter decisions about sustainability by offering advanced tools for managing carbon and other ESG data, and meeting the requirements of the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD).
“This partnership is an important step forward,” said
Jaco van Zijll Langhout, Partner at BearingPoint
. “It’s about connecting data-driven insights with action to create a lasting positive impact on the environment.” Sustainability is more than a box to tick— it’s becoming a core part of how businesses operate and plan for the future. But with new regulations like the CSRD and the Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD), companies face increasing pressure to stay transparent, accountable, and proactive.
Why this partnership matters
Together, BearingPoint and Sweep make it easier for organizations to manage their sustainability data, turn compliance into meaningful action, and create lasting business value. Sweep’s powerful platform simplifies the collection, analysis, and reporting of sustainability metrics. At the same time, BearingPoint’s expertise ensures that these solutions are tailored to each client’s needs, helping them build robust sustainability strategies and achieve ambitious goals – like Science-Based carbon reduction targets.
“We are pleased to partner with BearingPoint, combining our technology with their extensive expertise in sustainability,” said Rachel Delacour, Co-founder and CEO at Sweep. “This collaboration enables us to support businesses to manage their sustainability data effectively, ensure regulatory compliance, and drive meaningful business transformation which will enable them to thrive in the low-carbon economy of the future. We are proud of the work that we have already achieved together across several geographies”.
How it works
The partnership combines Sweep’s advanced technology with BearingPoint’s sustainability and transformation expertise:
Sweep’s platform
makes managing carbon and ESG data easy with AI-powered tools and a standardized, adaptable approach tailored to specific business needs.
BearingPoint’s role
ensures that this technology integrates seamlessly into each client’s operations, building capabilities, delivering real-world results and
supporting with the end-to-end sustainability journey.
Shared values
BearingPoint and Sweep are both proud to be certified B Corporations, reflecting their shared commitment to social and environmental responsibility.
By working together, they aim to empower businesses to take control of their sustainability journeys, and turn data into opportunity by meeting regulatory demands, and creating a greener, more sustainable future.
About BearingPoint
BearingPoint is an independent management and technology consulting firm with European roots and a global reach. The company operates in three business units: Consulting, Products and Capital. Consulting comprises consulting activities with a clear focus on selected business areas. Products provides IP-driven digital applications and managed services for mission-critical processes. Capital provides M&A and transaction services.
BearingPoint’s clients include many of the world’s leading companies and organizations. The company has a global consulting network of more than 10,000 people and supports clients in more than 70 countries, working with them to achieve measurable and sustainable success.
BearingPoint is a certified B Corporation, meeting high standards on social and environmental impact.
About Sweep
Sweep
is the sustainability data management platform. Its market-leading, AI-powered software helps organizations understand all extra-financial data across their business and value chain to manage increasing disclosure requirements and take action to meet sustainable business goals.
Co-founded by Rachel Delacour, Yannick Chaze, and Raphael Guller, Sweep partners with enterprise, midmarket and financial institutions across the world, with customers including L’Oreal, QVC, and Hewlett Packard.
Sweep is B Corp certified and a member of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition, France Invest and The International Emissions Trading Association.
