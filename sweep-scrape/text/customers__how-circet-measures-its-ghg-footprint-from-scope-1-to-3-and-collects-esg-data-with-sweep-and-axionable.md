Challenge & Solution
A global player in telecoms and energy infrastructure services,
Circet has expanded the management of its carbon footprint to 9 of the group’s 13 countries, leveraging Axionable’s expertise and the SWEEP solution.
This initiative aims
to structure and automate the measurement and monitoring of emissions while integrating historical data for a consolidated and reliable view.
One of Circet’s priorities is the automation of emission factor allocation on procurement data (Scope 3.1). Circet is also pursuing its ambition
to structure carbon governance by client, providing a detailed view of the carbon footprint for each entity of the group and each client.
This approach enables the
optimization of decarbonization strategies and the adaptation of actions at the scale of each partnership.
The use of SWEEP and the support of Axionable in this initiative have enabled Circet
to consolidate its carbon measurements and to engage all involved stakeholders.
Methodology and Tools
Use of international standards GHG Protocol (Scope 1, 2, & 3)
Rollout of the Sweep sustainability data management platform
Sweep’s Carbon module: 2023 carbon footprint, integration of historical data for 9 countries
Sweep’s Supply Chain module: supplier engagement
Results
A comprehensive carbon measurement
A thorough assessment of Scopes 1, 2, & 3 for the year 2023, complementing previous years’ measurements for 9 of the 13 countries within the Group
Full engagement of the Purchases line
Raw procurement data is fully integrated, with Emission factors and the Sweep branch automatically allocated
Centralization of carbon data
A centralized collection of carbon data across all 9 countries of operation
