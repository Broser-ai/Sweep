Unlocking business value from nonfinancial data for American companies

Last updated

Press release
Press release, 15th September 2025
Sweep, a leading platform in sustainability data management, is
expanding its U.S. footprint
with the launch of its first American office in Denver, Colorado. This strategic expansion represents a major milestone in Sweep’s North American growth trajectory, strategically placing the company at the heart of the country’s surging demand for advanced non-financial data management solutions.
Trusted by some of the most influential names in US business – including Crocs, CH Robinson and QCells  – Sweep’s platform turns sustainability data into tangible business wins. Sweep’s technology is enabling ambitious companies to drive efficiency, unlock new value, and compete in a rapidly evolving business and regulatory landscape. Sweep quickly maps carbon footprints, automates complex reporting, and builds actionable transition strategies for the world’s leading companies.
Denver was selected as Sweep’s US base for its unique combination of coast-to-coast accessibility, thriving technology ecosystem, and access to top-tier talent. The city’s dynamic business environment and central location provide an ideal launchpad for Sweep’s continued expansion across North America.
Sweep’s CEO and cofounder Rachel Delacour says:
“Recognition of our unique expertise in European regulations, particularly the CSRD, is reflected in a growing base of international clients, particularly in the United States. Forward-thinking international companies understand that sustainability is a strategic lever, as are the valuable non-financial data that result from it. The companies we work with are seeing the benefits, whether it’s reducing costs, better managing current and future risks, or optimizing their business model, especially their supply chain.”
Sweep’s Chief Revenue Officer and North America lead, Freddie House adds:
“Nonfinancial data is not just about compliance, it is a lever for profitability and resiliency. US businesses see them as a strategic asset, and this is why we are seeing strong growth in this market. Sweep is optimised to support businesses in all sectors – from energy to logistics, manufacturing to finance – to set the foundations today which will permit them to survive and thrive in the low-carbon economy of tomorrow”
Dr. Jeannette zu Fürstenberg, Managing Director at General Catalyst and Founding Partner of La Famiglia VC, commented:
“Market demand for digital tools that turn extra-financial data into business value is accelerating. The most forward-looking companies are already using this data as a strategic lever for resilience and competitiveness.
Sweep is leading this transformation. From the early days, we believed in their vision, and it’s inspiring to see the team now expand into the U.S., bringing their essential platform to an even broader set of organizations.”
About Sweep
Sweep: Strategic sustainability, powered by data
Sweep is the enterprise sustainability data management platform trusted by the world’s leading companies and financial institutions. Our SaaS solution helps organizations meet complex reporting requirements, map environmental impact across their value chain, and strategically implement sustainability strategies that meet business goals.
Sweep delivers transparent, granular, and actionable data-first insights. Our platform empowers decision-makers to identify regulatory and climate risks, design robust ESG transition plans, and build long-term business resilience.
From midmarket players to global giants like L’Oréal, QVC, and Lacoste, our clients use Sweep to embed sustainability at the core of their operations and stay competitive in a rapidly evolving regulatory and economic landscape.
Sweep has been recognized by Verdantix, IDC MarketScape, and MEDEF as a leader in sustainability data management.
Sweep CEO Rachel Delacour is an award-winning repeat entrepreneur with longstanding experience in the US market. In 2009, she cofounded BIME Analytics, a pioneering business intelligence SaaS which was acquired by US customer service company Zendesk six years later. Rachel worked there as General Manager until leaving to co-found Sweep alongside Yannick Chaze and Raphael Guller.
Sweep is B Corp certified and active members of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition, France Invest, and the International Emissions Trading Association.
