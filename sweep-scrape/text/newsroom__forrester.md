Forrester highlights Sweep among world’s top sustainability management software vendors
Last updated
September 24, 2024
News announcement
Leading analysts
Forrester
have highlighted Sweep as one of the world’s top 25 sustainability management software programs.
In the recent Sustainability Management Software Landscape Q1 report by Forrester, the French-founded SaaS platform was evaluated against the top use cases for the market: sustainability data collection and management, carbon accounting and calculation, reporting and disclosure, and ESG risk management.
Underscoring the “muddied” choices for buyers seeking comprehensive sustainability management software, Forrester selected Sweep among the leading 25 vendors worldwide.
Sweep’s CEO and co-founder Rachel Delacour said
“Companies that understand and track their carbon footprints and ESG performance will be those that survive the challenges of tomorrow.  Tackling our climate impact is a data project and companies must be equipped with a platform that will bring real insights and results. Sweep is that platform. I’m very happy that Forrester has recognized us as one of the world’s leading sustainability management software.
About Sweep
Sweep is the all-in-one platform to help organizations act on carbon and ESG data – from mapping to measuring, reducing to reporting. It’s the enterprise-ready solution to help companies scale climate action across teams, supply chains, and portfolio quickly and cost-effectively.
Cofounded by Rachel Delacour, Yannick Chaze and Raphael Gueller, Sweep is B Corp certified and a member of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition, France Invest, and The International Emissions Trading Association. It was selected to join FrenchTech Next 120/40 program and was named “Europe’s fastest growing scale-up” by Financial Times’ Sifted.
About Forrester
Forrester (Nasdaq: FORR) is one of the most influential research and advisory firms in the world. Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
