Sustainability initiatives face increasing pressure as regulations shift, budgets tighten, and leadership demands tangible results. Join our expert-led webinar series to learn how forward-thinking companies are transforming climate challenges into strategic opportunities.
Hear from industry leaders including former UNEP FI Head of Risk David Carlin and Elodie Broad, Head of Impact & ESG at Balderton. In addition to Sweep’s CEO and cofounder Rachel Delacour and Chief Impact Officer Julien Denormandie, and more.
Episode 1: Climate Risk Playbook: how to manage i
ncreasing climate risk to your business
Watch the recording now
In today’s challenging economic climate, climate risk is a direct threat to your bottom line. From rising costs an
d supply chain disruption to investor pressure and regulatory scrutiny, the financial risks of inaction are growing fast. Companies that delay are exposed to capital loss, reputational damage, and litigation.
In this webinar, Sweep’s Chief Impact Officer, Julien Denormandie, and David Carlin—former Head of Risk at the UN Environment Programme Finance Initiative (UNEP FI)— explored key strategies to help businesses manage climate risk across their value chain.
In this session, you’ll learn how to:
Identify and respond to financial and operational climate risks
Stay ahead of regulation and disclosure requirements
Build resilience into your business strategy

Episode 2: Proving the business value of sustainability efforts
Watch the recording now:
With climate regulations across Europe and the US are in a state of flux, and political headwinds are causing volatility to global trade and policy stability, sustainability projects (and the teams behind them) aren’t as high on agendas. But for businesses, sustainability projects remain mission and bottom line critical. Proving the business ROI of sustainability initiatives isn’t just a matter of compliance or corporate responsibility—it’s about showing how climate action delivers real business value, can cut costs, make operations more efficient and much more.
In this webinar Rachel Delacour, CEO & Cofounder of Sweep, and Elodie Broad, Head of Impact and ESG at Balderton, to learn key strategies on how you can show the financial contribution of sustainability initiatives and strengthen your role as a strategic driver of performance.
