Sweep makes sustainability reporting simpler
With regulations like the CSRD, SEC, and ISSB frameworks, companies must now track and disclose environmental, social, and governance (ESG) data with the same rigor as financial reporting. That means managing thousands of data points from different data sets and systems across business units, suppliers, and markets, often under tight deadlines.
Traditional tools were not designed for this. Teams get stuck chasing missing inputs, fixing errors, or consolidating data from dozens of different systems. The result is delays, inconsistencies, and frustrated employees.
This is where Sweep’s agentic AI makes the difference. Purpose-built for the sustainability domain, it does more than crunch numbers. It interprets them, validates them, and helps you transform them into the insights and disclosures you need, faster and with greater confidence.
Sweepy, your AI co-pilot for sustainability
At the heart of our approach is Sweepy, our AI that works across the entire platform. It coordinates specialized AI agents that work behind the scenes to help you make the most of the Sweep platform, generate instant insights, and orchestrate the right support at the right time. Whether you need to build a dashboard, close a data gap, or draft a disclosure, Sweepy ensures you do not waste time searching for tools or instructions.
Think of it as your sustainability co-pilot: always ready, always reliable, and always focused on impact.
Human-in-the-loop
AI should never remove control from the people who use it. Every action in Sweep is
human-in-the-loop
, meaning you review, approve, and stay in control of the decisions that matter most. Our AI is there to support you, not replace you or your teams.
This approach guarantees that your sustainability data remains accurate and auditable, while keeping you compliant with evolving standards. You benefit from automation and intelligence without losing control.
What businesses gain with our agentic AI
Efficiency without compromise
Automate data collection, visualisation, and reporting tasks so your teams can focus on strategy and impact.
Audit-ready confidence
Every AI-augmented action comes with traceability and transparency. You get a clear audit trail, built-in validation checks, and reliable disclosures.
Inclusive collaboration
Sustainability is increasingly cross-functional. Sweep makes it easy for finance, HR, procurement, and operations to contribute without needing technical expertise.
Faster performance
With AI-powered workflows, organizations can accelerate time-to-insights, reduce reporting costs, and act on opportunities for climate action sooner.
Why Sweep’s AI is different
Unlike off-the-shelf AI tools, Sweep’s agentic AI is
domain-specific
. It is powered by curated domain information, including emission factors, up-to-date regulatory frameworks, and sustainability best practices, not generic internet data.
And because it is fully embedded in Sweep’s Interoperable Data Fabric, there is no extra setup, migration, or learning curve. You get intelligence exactly where and when you need it, in the context of your existing workflows.
From compliance to action
Agentic AI delivers a lot of value beyond simply meeting reporting requirements. It unlocks the true business value of sustainability. With Sweep, your CSRD or ESG disclosures become the foundation for smarter decision-making: increasing supplier participation, setting science-based targets, modeling impact scenarios, and tracking progress toward net zero.
By embedding intelligence directly into your workflows, Sweep turns sustainability from a compliance burden into a driver of growth and resilience.
The future of sustainability management with Sweep
AI will continue to shape the way businesses operate. For sustainability leaders, the real question is: will it help you move faster and with more confidence toward your climate goals?
With Sweep’s approach to agentic AI, Sweepy, the answer is yes. You spend less time managing data and more time acting on it, unlocking measurable environmental impact.
