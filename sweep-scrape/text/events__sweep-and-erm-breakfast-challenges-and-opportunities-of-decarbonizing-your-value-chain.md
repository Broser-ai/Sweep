Sweep & ERM Breakfast: Challenges and Opportunities of Decarbonizing Your Value Chain
Join ERM and Sweep on April 25th, 2024, at Fora Spitalfields, London, for a breakfast session on value chain decarbonization, where we'll tackle Scope 3 challenges and explore ways to boost sustainability and business growth.

Reducing the carbon footprint of your value chain is challenging.
Sweep, the sustainability data management platform, and ERM are hosting an exclusive breakfast event on Scope 3 decarbonization. We’ll be discussing how to leverage the power of collaboration and granular data management to turn value chain decarbonization challenges into business opportunities.
At this event you will learn more about:
Increasing stakeholder engagement and collaboration
Building resilient supply chains
Uncovering opportunities to grow your business
Key stakeholders such as suppliers, buyers as well as private equity funds and portfolio companies,  will share insights on the power of digitalization and collaboration.

8:30 – Welcome and coffee
9:00 – Roundtable
