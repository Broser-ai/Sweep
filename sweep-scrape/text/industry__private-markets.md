Private market participants face increasing LP expectations
LPs now demand a rigorous measurement of carbon emissions for private market portfolio companies, in line with industry imperatives, and regulatory mandates. Navigating this landscape means reporting to global standards such as the GHG Protocol and SFDR. That’s why private equity firms, VCs and hedge funds need the right software to manage the process effectively.
Book a demo
Diverse and complex portfolios
Private market firms oversee a mix of portfolio companies at different stages of maturity, meaning they must ensure comprehensive emissions data collection.
Data availability
Data availability poses a challenge for many private equity firms, given the inconsistencies and gaps in ESG data across diverse portfolios.
Regulatory complexity
Private market players must navigate a complex web of ever-evolving global regulations related to carbon emissions and ESG disclosures.
75% of private equity investors said they assess ESG materiality for individual companies.
