EVENT
Manage your Impact on Sweep
Due to increasing stakeholder pressure, many companies have set or are in the process of setting robust science-based targets. Regardless of where you stand, Sweep allows you to set or import existing targets, and build a dynamic strategy to identify how you'll reach your goals.
In this webinar, you will learn how to use the Improve section in Sweep to set impactful targets and build your improvement strategy.
We'll also share our best practices, recommendations, and guidelines to provide you with a clear framework for building your strategy.
