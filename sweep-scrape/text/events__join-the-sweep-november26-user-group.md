The Sweep User Group Program is a customer-focused initiative. Its purpose is to encourage connection, exchange, and innovation, enabling you in enhancing your sustainability performance.
Share solutions for common challenges:
Every customer faces challenges. Use our program as a platform to share practical solutions and learn from others.
Customer-to-customer support:
Need advice or have insights to share? Our “Customer-to-Customer” initiative helps you provide and receive support from the community.
Exclusive access & feedback loop:

Expand your knowledge:
Networking opportunities and industry insights can help you get the most from your Sweep investment. Grow your knowledge, save time, and reach your goals faster
