**Description:** Sweep is the sustainability intelligence platform that turns sustainability data into measurable business performance.

We’ve raised a $22m Series A to scale collective corporate climate action
Last updated
June 04, 2025
News announcement
We’re accelerating our mission to empower companies and their partners with carbon data, so they take impactful and immediate steps towards decarbonisation.
When I see the immensity of our climate challenge, it’s obvious that no one can do this alone.
To reach planetary net-zero, we need collaboration at scale.
That’s why we’ve built Sweep to help large companies measure and reduce emissions, together with their teams and value chains. To bring transparency and accuracy to an activity that
isn’t there yet.
And to shift the focus from just measuring to actually taking action. Together.
We could not do this without the support of our very own network. From our early customers who trusted us since we
launched in April
, to our partners in the tech and climate industries, to our all-star team.
This also holds true for our investors. Today, I’m honoured to announce that we have raised a $22m Series A led by
Balderton
and with funding from our existing investors
New Wave
,
La Famiglia
and
2050
.
We’re so excited to see that there are more and more companies committed to tackling their climate mess. And we’re ready to help you.
With such a stellar community by our side, we will turbocharge our enterprise-ready solution that offers a science-based, actionable and connected approach to carbon management.
We’re so excited to see that there are more and more companies committed to tackling their climate mess. And we’re ready to help you.
It’s time to join the network of change.
Rachel Delacour

Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
