# 2026 insights featuring Forrester: Navigating the new sustainability landscape and AI’s growing role - Sweep


**Description:** Sweep’s CRO Freddie House, and guest speaker Abhijit Sunil, Senior Analyst at Forrester, look at what's driving success in the year ahead, based on key learnings from 2025.

The sustainability landscape is evolving rapidly. As we look toward 2026, three critical trends are reshaping how organizations approach their sustainability strategies – and the teams that get ahead of these changes will have a competitive advantage.
Featured speakers:
Abhijit Sunil, Senior Analyst, Forrester
Freddie House, CRO, Sweep
What you’ll discover:
Regulatory evolution
: How the fragmented regulatory landscape is creating new opportunities for interoperability, and practical strategies for staying compliant while capitalizing on emerging standards
AI’s expanding role in sustainability
: Use cases where artificial intelligence is delivering measurable impact for sustainability teams, plus the implementation challenges you need to navigate
The importance of ROI
: How the  increasing pressure on sustainability teams to demonstrate business value empowers leading organizations to make the shift from compliance-focused to performance-driven approaches
