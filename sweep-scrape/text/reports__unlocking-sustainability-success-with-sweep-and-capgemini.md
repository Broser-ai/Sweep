ESG management
Companies across industries are facing increasing pressure to meet regulatory requirements, align with global environmental goals, and contribute to a low-carbon future. At Sweep, we believe that the key to this transformation lies in leveraging data, technology, and strategic partnerships to address challenges such as climate change and biodiversity loss.
That’s why one of our strategic partnerships is with Capgemini, to provide businesses with the tools and guidance they need to tackle their sustainability challenges head-on. Together, we offer solutions that combine our expertise in sustainability data management with Capgemini’s global consulting experience, to empower organizations to drive meaningful change.
Understanding the role of businesses in sustainable development
Sustainable development is essential to creating a world where economic growth, social inclusion, and environmental protection work together to ensure long-term viability. Popularized by the 1987 Brundtland Commission report, it emphasizes the need for individuals to meet their own needs without compromising the ability of future generations to do the same. For businesses, this concept goes beyond compliance—it’s about driving lasting change that benefits both society and the planet.
By integrating sustainability into their core operations, companies have the power to influence entire supply chains, reduce carbon emissions, and foster innovation for greener solutions. To make this transition, company leaders must look at more than just the immediate returns. They need to adopt a long-term perspective, balancing profitability with the responsibility to protect ecosystems, support local communities, and promote ethical labor practices.
This holistic approach is key to creating resilient business models that thrive in a low-carbon economy while contributing to the well-being of society. However, navigating the complexities of sustainability requires businesses to manage vast amounts of data, meet evolving regulatory requirements, and maintain transparency with stakeholders.
The power of data in driving sustainability
According to a recent Sweep x Capgemini survey, 83% of financial institutions still use Excel spreadsheets to track emissions data, and nearly half report that their sustainability data is not comprehensive enough to inform strategic decisions.
This lack of visibility can prevent companies from taking the decisive actions needed to meet their carbon reduction goals.
At Sweep, we recognize that data is at the heart of sustainability transformation. Companies need precise, actionable information to navigate today’s regulatory environment, meet disclosure requirements, and create a comprehensive sustainability strategy
Sweep simplifies the complexity of managing vast amounts of extra-financial data, automating the process of tracking emissions across a business and its value chain.
By consolidating and analyzing data, we bridge the physical and digital worlds, helping companies understand and act on their sustainability data, ensuring compliance with global standards such as the GHG Protocol and CSRD.
Sweep and Capgemini’s partnership
Sweep and Capgemini’s partnership aims to help clients achieve their sustainability goals by providing them with access to Sweep’s market-leadingsustainability platform and Capgemini’s globally-recognized expertise in sustainability consulting. Together, we are committed to driving meaningful business decarbonization.
Our joint solutions include:
Sustainable supply chain management
: Together, we help clients optimize their supply chains to be more sustainable, reducing waste and improving resource efficiency.
Green technologies and innovation
: Leveraging advanced technologies, we drive innovation in sustainability, and enable businesses to identify emission hotspots.
Sustainable business models and circular economy
: We assist clients in developing business models that prioritize sustainability and embrace the principles of the circular economy.
Environmental sustainability and pollution reduction
: Our strategies focus on reducing environmental pollution and promoting sustainable practices.
Social sustainability and human rights
: We advocate for social sustainability by ensuring fair labor practices and supporting human rights initiatives.
Overcoming barriers to sustainability
Achieving sustainability goals can be challenging due to various barriers, including lack of corporate awareness, limited resources, and inadequate infrastructure, especially in developing countries. These regions face specific challenges such as limited resources and inadequate infrastructure, which hinder their ability to meet contemporary development needs without compromising future generations. To overcome these obstacles, businesses must adopt a holistic approach that considers the interconnectedness of economic, social, and environmental factors. This approach requires a long-term perspective, focusing on the sustainable use of natural resources and the reduction of environmental pollution.
Capgemini’s expertise in sustainability is instrumental in helping businesses navigate these challenges. The company’s more than 350,000 strategic and technological experts across 50 countries offer a range of services, including sustainability strategy development, sustainability reporting, and supply chain optimization, to support organizations in their sustainability journey. By addressing these barriers, Capgemini enables businesses to achieve sustainable development and contribute to a sustainable future.
Building a sustainable future
At Sweep, we believe that businesses have the potential to shape a sustainable future for both their organizations and the planet. Our partnership with Capgemini allows us to take that belief a step further by offering businesses a comprehensive approach to sustainability—one that combines powerful data management technology with deep consulting expertise.
As the world moves towards a low-carbon economy, the decisions that companies make today will impact not only their bottom line but also their legacy. Those that embrace sustainability as a core part of their strategy will be better positioned to thrive in a future where environmental responsibility is a business imperative. Together with Capgemini, we are helping organizations meet the challenges of today while building a sustainable tomorrow for the global community and future generations.
About Sweep
Sweep is a leading sustainability data management platform that empowers organizations across the globe to adopt sustainable practices and manage their environmental impact. With its AI-powered software, Sweep enables company leaders to collect, analyze, and act on extra-financial data across their entire value chain, ensuring compliance with evolving regulations and disclosure requirements.
By focusing on actionable insights and transparency, Sweep helps enterprise, mid- and small businesses and financial organizations drive positive effects on both their operations and the planet. Sweep partners with global enterprises, including L’Oréal, Lacoste, and Hewlett Packard, and is a proud member of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition.
About Capgemini
Capgemini is a global leader in consulting, digital transformation, and technology services, dedicated to helping businesses address environmental problems and integrate sustainable practices into their operations through sustainability consulting. The company’s approach to sustainability is built around three pillars: environmental sustainability, social sustainability (diversity, inclusion and human rights), and economic development. It operates on the belief that economic development is essential for achieving sustainable development.
Capgemini’s focus on strategy, engineering, and data-driven insights enables businesses to modernize their operations and enhance their sustainability goals. By fostering partnerships and leveraging advanced technologies such as AI, Capgemini ensures clients achieve lasting positive effects while addressing key environmental and business challenges.
