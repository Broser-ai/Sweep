From Europe to North America: What’s next for sustainability?
Philippe Zaouati – CEO & Founder, Mirova
A pioneer in responsible investing, Philippe will share his vision for the future of sustainable finance.
Isabelle Hudon – President & CEO, BDC (Business Development Bank of Canada)
From her former role as Canadian Ambassador to France to leading BDC, Isabelle will provide her North American perspective on the state of sustainability.
Julien Denormandie – Chief Impact Officer, Sweep
Former French Minister of Agriculture, Julien will analyze current regulatory developments at the European level.
