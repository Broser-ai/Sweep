Sweep Review for ESG and Sustainability Management
See how Sweep helps organizations manage sustainability reporting and tie this to business performance with its all-in-one sustainability intelligence platform
Category
Blog
Last updated
May 21, 2026
Sustainability data is increasingly being used beyond reporting and compliance. Organizations are now relying on sustainability data to understand operational inefficiencies, manage supply chain risk, support investment decisions, and more directly connect sustainability initiatives to business performance.
As a result, many organizations are looking for systems that can connect carbon data, ESG reporting, supplier engagement, operational workflows, and sustainability strategy within a single environment.
Sweep is positioned around this broader, all-in-one approach to sustainability management.
This article explores how Sweep approaches sustainability management, the capabilities it offers across reporting and operational workflows, and where it fits within the broader sustainability software landscape.
What is Sweep and who is it best suited for?
Sweep is a
sustainability intelligence platform
designed to help organizations connect sustainability data with reporting, operational workflows, and business decision-making.
The platform is built around the idea that sustainability data should support more than disclosures alone. Alongside carbon accounting and ESG reporting, Sweep helps organizations manage Scope 3 emissions, supplier engagement, financed emissions, operational sustainability initiatives, and cross-functional sustainability coordination within a single system.
Sweep is typically best suited to organizations managing complex sustainability programs across multiple teams, entities, reporting frameworks, and operational environments. This includes organizations looking to:
Centralize carbon and ESG data across reporting and operational workflows
Manage Scope 3 emissions
and supplier engagement at scale
Reuse sustainability data across multiple disclosure frameworks and reporting requirements
Improve visibility into
financed emissions
and portfolio sustainability performance
Connect sustainability initiatives with operational efficiency, risk management, and business strategy
This approach allows organizations to move beyond disconnected reporting processes and build a more integrated sustainability management system across the business.
Sweep’s core features and capabilities
Carbon and ESG data management
Sweep provides a centralized environment for
managing carbon and ESG data
across teams, entities, and reporting processes. The platform is designed to reduce reliance on disconnected spreadsheets and manual reporting workflows by creating a more consistent sustainability data foundation across the organization.
This helps organizations maintain greater visibility, consistency, and coordination across sustainability reporting and operational activities.
Scope 3 and supply chain visibility
Scope 3 management
is a major focus within the platform. Sweep supports supplier data collection, value chain emissions tracking, and ongoing collaboration across supply chain sustainability workflows.
Organizations can use the platform to improve visibility into supplier emissions data, identify sustainability risks across the value chain, and support broader supplier engagement and decarbonization initiatives.
Disclosure and reporting workflows
Sweep supports sustainability disclosures across
multiple reporting frameworks
while allowing organizations to reuse underlying sustainability data across reporting requirements.
The platform includes validation workflows, governance controls, reporting templates, and audit-ready traceability designed to improve consistency and reduce manual reporting work across sustainability disclosures.
Financed emissions measurement
Sweep also includes capabilities for
financed emissions measurement
and portfolio-level sustainability visibility. This supports organizations managing investment-related emissions reporting and broader sustainability analysis across portfolios, assets, and financed activities.
These capabilities are particularly relevant for financial institutions, investors, and organizations managing financed emissions disclosure requirements.
Data-driven sustainability strategy
Beyond reporting, Sweep is designed to help organizations
connect sustainability data with operational and strategic decision-making
. The platform supports visibility into operational inefficiencies, sustainability performance drivers, and opportunities to prioritize initiatives based on business impact and ROI.
This helps sustainability data become more actionable across operational planning and long-term strategy development.
Sweep AI and workflow automation
Sweep includes
agentic AI capabilities
designed to automate repetitive sustainability workflows and improve reporting efficiency across teams.
The platform supports automated quality checks, workflow traceability, and AI-assisted collaboration, helping organizations
accelerate reporting processes
while maintaining governance and transparency across sustainability operations.
Sweep in action
SSE, a multinational energy company with more than 12,000 employees, implemented Sweep to
improve sustainability data management, audit readiness, and CSRD preparation
across its operations.
Before adopting Sweep, SSE relied heavily on spreadsheets, emails, and manual data collection processes across multiple business units. This created challenges around data consistency, reporting accuracy, auditability, and visibility into sustainability performance across the organization.
Sweep was implemented to help SSE:
Centralize climate and sustainability data within a single source of truth
Reduce spreadsheet dependency and manual reporting workflows
Improve auditability and reporting confidence across sustainability disclosures
Track emissions performance against science-based targets over time
Improve visibility into reporting gaps ahead of CSRD compliance requirements
Support more frequent sustainability data collection and operational monitoring
Using Sweep, SSE was able to consolidate multiple years of emissions data, improve collaboration with auditors, and provide sustainability teams and senior leadership with more consistent visibility into emissions hotspots and reporting performance.

Where Sweep fits within the sustainability software landscape
Sustainability platforms are increasingly being designed around different operational priorities.
Some focus primarily on carbon accounting and emissions measurement, while others are centered on ESG reporting, enterprise data governance, or climate strategy and decarbonization planning.
Sweep is positioned within a broader category of sustainability intelligence platforms, where sustainability data is connected not only to reporting but also to operational workflows, supplier engagement, and business decision-making.
Sustainability platform category
Typical focus
Carbon accounting platforms
Emissions measurement, Scope 1–3 reporting, and climate disclosures
ESG reporting platforms
Governance, disclosures, auditability, and regulatory reporting workflows
Enterprise ESG data platforms
Centralized ESG data management, reporting infrastructure, and governance
Climate intelligence platforms
Emissions analysis, decarbonization planning, and climate strategy
Sustainability intelligence platforms
Connecting sustainability data with operational workflows, reporting, supplier collaboration, and business performance
This shift reflects how sustainability management is evolving inside organizations.
Sustainability data is increasingly expected to support operational planning, supply chain visibility, cost-reduction initiatives, and long-term business strategy, as well as reporting and compliance requirements.
Turning sustainability data into business performance
As sustainability programs mature, many organizations are looking for systems that can support both disclosure requirements and ongoing operational decision-making across the business.
Sweep is designed around this approach to sustainability management, helping organizations connect carbon and ESG data with reporting workflows, supplier engagement, financed emissions analysis, and operational sustainability initiatives within a single platform.
