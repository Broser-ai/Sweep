**Description:** Sweep is the sustainability intelligence platform that turns sustainability data into measurable business performance.

We are a B Corp!
Last updated
December 11, 2025
News announcement
Sweep is now part of the B Corp community. Our certification will hold us accountable as we scale our team and carbon management platform. Here’s how we want to have an impact in 2022.
When we launched Sweep with Nicolas, Yannick and Raphael, we knew we wanted to build for impact and with impact. But it’s easier to do so when you run a startup. It becomes more challenging as you grow. There are more variables to account for, compromises to make and not enough time to do it all.
So we took a couple of steps to match our values with our big ambitions – and hold us accountable:
👉 Finding the right people: We carefully surrounded ourselves with team members, investors and partners who share our vision – building Sweep for business leaders who want to seriously deal with their climate mess and build a better future.
👉 Register as a French “
societe a mission
”: In addition to our investor board, we have a mission committee to advise us as we
grow Sweep
. It includes leading sustainability figures UTOPIES’
Elisabeth Laville
, ex-Engie
Ana Busto
, Projections CC’s
Vanessa Laubin
, Shine’s
Nicolas Reboud
and Sweep’s
Renaud Bettin
.
👉 Become a B Corp: It’s an extra check to ensure that we operate in a way that best benefits our team, our community and the planet. We’re proud to be involved in a forward-thinking business community with the likes of
Patagonia
,
Danone
and WeTransfer.
Together, we are using business as a ‘force for good.’ It’s the only way forward to manage the climate crisis on our hands.
Our impact goals for 2022
One of B Corp’s key values is transparency, so I’m going to share our three impact goals for 2022. And I’d encourage you to do the same.
🌎
Mind our own footprint on the planet
: We educate our team via weekly webinars, track our emissions through Sweep (duh) and set reduction targets.
🧑🏾‍🤝‍🧑🏻
Maintain a safe and healthy work environment as we grow
:
Kevin
recently joined us to nurture our remote culture and support employee growth.
🎁
Support business leaders on their climate journeys
: Through learning resources, meetups, calls, we’re here to
answer all your questions
about carbon management and climate finance.
What climate initiatives does your team want to implement in 2022?
Let’s talk
.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
