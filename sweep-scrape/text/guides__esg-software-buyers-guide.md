The smart way to evaluate ESG tools
ESG software should help you go beyond reporting: giving you the insights and controls to reduce risk, optimize operations, and drive change.
In a world where every claim is being scrutinized, by investors, regulators, customers, and your own employees, you need proof that your efforts are real, tangible, and value-driven. ESG software gives you that proof, and the best platforms go further:
they help you find hidden risks, unlock efficiency, and turn sustainability into a genuine business advantage
.
Disclosure is one part of the job. But the real value lies in what you do with the data, and how confidently you can act on it.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
