Reporting frameworks
With the CSRD approaching, over 3,000 US companies operating in the EU must prepare for new regulations that enhance transparency in environmental, social, and governance (ESG) reporting.
This webinar breaks down everything you need to know about:
Understanding the CSRD, its goals, key metrics, and the risks of non-compliance.
Identifying which US businesses must comply with EU reporting rules and practical steps for effective data management.
How early compliance can offer a competitive edge and the importance of knowing when to disclose data to avoid penalties.
Watch the replay now
featuring experts Julien Denormandie, Chief Impact Officer at Sweep, and Laura Xu, Senior Product Manager at Arcadia, as they share actionable insights on how the CSRD will impact US companies.
