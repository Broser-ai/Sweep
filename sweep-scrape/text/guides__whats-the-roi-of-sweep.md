Sustainability ROI
At Sweep, we understand that many firms struggle to build their business case for sustainable data management software. Too often, CEOs rely on outdated tools like spreadsheets, that limit their ability to unlock the true potential of sustainability initiatives.
That’s why we partnered with Verdantix to conduct an in-depth study – using their Verified Value Delivery (VVD) methodology to evaluate Sweep’s financial, strategic, and risk-mitigating benefits.
What’s inside the report summary?
The report provides a clear, fact-based assessment of Sweep’s impact, grounded in real customer experiences and robust financial modeling.
How the study was conducted:
Verdantix’s VVD methodology includes in-depth customer interviews, financial modeling, and an analysis of strategic benefits. This rigorous process ensures the findings are data-backed and actionable.
Key findings:
Sweep users benefit from:
238% ROI
over three years
€1.23M in total benefits
€864K net present value (NPV)
over three years
A
9-month break-even point
, showing rapid value realization
The three lenses of evaluation:
Financial:
Documenting measurable cost savings and financial upside
Strategic:
Highlighting how Sweep helps achieve key business goals
Risk:
Identifying risks in ESG management and recommending mitigation strategies
.
