Sweep Named a Leader in 2026 IDC MarketScape for Carbon Accounting and Management Applications, Scoring Highest Among 17 Global Vendors
Top ranking reflects platform's ability to turn sustainability data into business value and resilience
Category
News Announcement
Last updated
April 30, 2026
Analyst report
News announcement
Sweep has been ranked highest among all 17 shortlisted vendors for both capabilities and strategy in the 2026 IDC MarketScape for Worldwide Carbon Accounting and Management Applications.
IDC identified Sweep as particularly well suited to organizations managing complex operations, large datasets, and multi-entity structures. The SaaS’ architecture supports high data accuracy, real-time validation, and consistent reporting across the business.
“Being ranked highest among our competitors by IDC reflects a strategic choice we made from the start: to build a platform that handles real organizational complexity at scale,  with the data quality, auditability and governance teams need to drive business performance,”
said Rachel Delacour, CEO of Sweep.
“Sustainability isn’t about compliance anymore. It’s about cost savings, risk reduction, and measurable results. IDC’s recognition confirms what we already see with our customers: when you get the data infrastructure right, you turn sustainability into a competitive advantage.”
Skills-based AI architecture
The report highlighted multiple key strengths, including Sweep’s skills-based AI architecture which is designed around a small number of highly capable, explainable agents, built on a trusted data foundation. AI now accounts for 40% of IDC’s strategy score, reflecting its importance to businesses as an accelerator of sustainability intelligence management.
Mastery of enterprise-grade data
IDC’s analysts underscored Sweep’s mastery of enterprise-grade data management at scale. The platform supports millions of rows of data with real-time validation and inconsistency tracking. A “load once, use everywhere” approach ensures a single dataset powers reporting, analysis, and decision-making.
Support for complex organization structures
Sweep also enables detailed mapping of complex organizational structures, including multi-entity, multi-site, and multi-subsidiary environments, with precise emissions allocation and aggregation.
LCA workflows
IDC also noted Sweep’s integrated Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) workflows, which reduce operational complexity when applied to Scope 3 emissions data. The report noted that one Sweep customer interviewed for the analysis reduced LCA completion time by 50% while lowering its reliance on external consultants.
The IDC MarketScape recognition underscores Sweep’s strong relevance to organizations facing increasing pressure to demonstrate both regulatory compliance and business value from their sustainability programs, in the face of economic and political headwinds.
Sweep continues to expand its platform capabilities to help customers worldwide turn sustainability data into strategic advantage.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.

Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
