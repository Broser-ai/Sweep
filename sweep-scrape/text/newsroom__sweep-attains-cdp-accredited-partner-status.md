News announcement
We are pleased to announce that Sweep has achieved Accredited Partner Silver status with the
CDP
(Carbon Disclosure Project). The recognition serves to recommend Sweep to the more than 22,000 companies which report to the CDP’s framework annually.
CDP is a not-for-profit charity that operates the global disclosure system used by investors, companies, cities, states, and regions to manage their environmental impacts, and is widely regarded as the gold standard for environmental reporting.
“Data is the foundation for building business resilience. It prepares businesses for the future by facilitating precise analysis, rigorous audits, a solid reputation and transparent reporting”
said Sweep’s CEO and cofounder Rachel Delacour
. “
We are pleased to support a growing list of businesses around the world in building and managing transparent, granular, and auditable datasets to both achieve successful CDP reporting and to transform this data into ongoing business benefits.”
Sweep provides a complete solution for CDP reporting, while cutting the time taken to complete it, in half. Our software uses organizations’ existing emissions data to automatically pre-fill the CDP questionnaire while Sweep’s unique AI agents not only help in drafting qualitative responses in as little as a few minutes, they also analyze responses and provide recommendations to boost CDP scores.
To maximise the value of this work,
Sweep automatically maps a user’s CDP data
to other reporting frameworks, including ISSB, GHG Protocol, CSRD,  GRI, SB 253, among others, while Sweep’s complete data traceability makes audits easier.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
