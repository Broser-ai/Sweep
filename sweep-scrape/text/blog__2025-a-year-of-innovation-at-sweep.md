2025: A year of innovation at Sweep
Discover how Sweep innovated in 2025 with AI, smarter reporting, and supply chain tools that turn sustainability data into confident decisions.
Category
Blog
Last updated
February 04, 2026
Reporting frameworks
ESG management
Supply chain emissions
2025 asked a lot of sustainability teams. Across industries, expectations kept shifting, timelines grew tighter, and teams were asked to deliver credible results with limited time and resources.
At Sweep, we stayed focused on one thing: listening closely to our customers’ needs and building practical improvements that make sustainability management easier and more valuable for businesses. Here are the highlights.
AI built for sustainability work
We heard the same frustration again and again: too much time spent on manual work, not enough on decisions. So we introduced domain-specific AI designed to take the heavy lifting off teams’ shoulders, without taking away control.
From messy data to information you can trust.
AI that helps structure, enrich, and validate sustainability data, so teams can spend less time cleaning and more time deciding.
From blank page to audit ready disclosure.
Automated drafting aligned with major reporting frameworks, grounded in your own documentation with clear source traceability.
Analytics without the bottlenecks.
Dashboards and formulas in plain language, giving non-technical teams direct access to insight.
AI support where the work actually happens.
Embedded assistance across surveys, disclosures, and analysis, helping teams move faster, and work with confidence.
Innovation you can stand behind.
Transparent, auditable AI with human oversight and visibility into environmental impact.
Supporting all your reporting needs from a single dataset
As reporting requirements multiplied, customers told us the hardest part was not understanding the frameworks, but keeping their data consistent and reusable.
We strengthened Sweep’s ability to turn one structured dataset into many confident disclosures:
Report once, respond everywhere.
Support for global and regional reporting standards from a single source of truth.
CSRD without the sprawl.
Structured libraries and scoring workflows that bring clarity and control to CSRD-related assessments.
Advancing supply chain sustainability
Supplier engagement continued to be one of the toughest challenges. Customers told us that collecting data was only the first step. What they really needed was direction.
Our focus was on turning supplier data into action so that users can:
Spot risks and opportunities quickly
through supplier benchmarking
Track real progress over time
, not just one off snapshots
Prioritize engagement
using external climate commitments
Test procurement decisions
by modeling emissions impact before making changes
Look beyond carbon
with broader ESG data collection
Engage suppliers globally
with multi-language surveys that improve response rates
Turning sustainability data into impact
Customers also wanted clearer ways to understand what their data was really telling them and how it connected to everyday business decisions.
We focused on making insight easier, so that our users could:
See progress over time
with clear year over year and time based analysis
Understand what drives emissions
by linking activity data directly to outcomes
Bring qualitative insight into view
alongside quantitative metrics
Explore data in different ways
with flexible filtering and formula based views
Plan with confidence
using tools for transition planning, scenarios, and initiative tracking
Looking ahead
As we move into the next year, we will continue building practical, resilient tools for real-world sustainability challenges. Our focus remains on deeper intelligence, thoughtful automation, and reporting that adapts as requirements evolve, always shaped by the needs of our customers.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
