Key Takeaways
Beaumanoir Group has set a target to reduce its greenhouse gas emissions by 30% by 2030
The group’s main challenge was consolidating and understanding emissions data, managing multiple brands in regions across the globe while acquiring new companies simultaneously
With Sweep, Beaumanoir Group is able to centralise emissions data from all of its brands, identify hotspots and set appropriate actions in place that will ensure the group hits its target by 2030
“Sweep allows us to consolidate all our greenhouse gas emissions into a single tool, and to differentiate them by brand”
Emeline Richard
CSR Project Manager
Background
Beaumanoir Group is a major player in fashion and lifestyle in France, with six global brands, including Cache Cache, Bréal, Bonobo, La Halle, Morgan, Caroll. It has more than 2,700 sales outlets.
Since June 2024, the Beaumanoir Group has been operating the Boardriders Group’s sportswear and outdoor brands for Western Europe.
In addition to its distribution business, the Group has France’s leading lifestyle marketplace Sarenza.com, and a logistics subsidiary, C-Log with 10 warehouses across Europe.
Challenge
Beaumanoir Group wanted to achieve a 30% reduction of its greenhouse gas emissions by 2030. Its major challenge was managing a broad scope of activity from logistics to resales with numerous brands and platforms.
The sustainability team gathered data from a wide range of departments and teams, which led to the data being sent in a variety of formats. These then had to be manually streamlined, meaning data collection was time-consuming and tedious.
The sustainability team needed a tool that would centralise and simplify the task, and help ensure the 30% emissions reduction target was met on time by 2030. This came at a time when Groupe Beaumanoir was acquiring new companies and regularly readjusting their emissions baseline.
Solution
With Sweep, Beaumanoir Group is able to consolidate all of its greenhouse gas emissions into a single tool. The platform also enables Beaumanoir’s sustainability team to differentiate emissions by brand, making it easy to identify hotspots of emissions, and focus efforts on making strategic reductions.
Sweep has also enabled the sustainability team to analyse its emissions data from one year to the next.
Sweep’s dashboards and “tree” visualisation make it easy to visualise and assess the effects of any emissions reduction actions.
Leveraging the Sweep platform means Groupe Beaumanoir can see clear progress reports and action trajectories, as CSR Project Manager Emeline Richard underlines
: “Above all it allows us to easily assess the carbon gains of the actions we are planning, would like to take, or are in the process of taking. So we can see how much work we still have to do and how we can achieve our objectives for 2030”
