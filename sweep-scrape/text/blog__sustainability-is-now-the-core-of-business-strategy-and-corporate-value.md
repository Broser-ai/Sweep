Sustainability is now core to business strategy. At VivaTech, Sweep and KPMG unpack why ESG is shaping competitiveness, deals, and long-term growth.
Category

Last updated
June 20, 2025
Sustainability ROI
At VivaTech 2025, our Chief Impact Officer and former French Agriculture Minister, Julien Denormandie joined Jérémie Joos, Partner at KPMG and Head of the ESG Center for Excellence, for a forward-looking conversation on where sustainability is really headed.
The focus was on how companies are turning ESG into a source of competitive advantage—and why those who don’t risk being left behind.
Regulatory shifts aren’t stopping business momentum
With CSRD changes in Europe and uncertainty around climate policy in the US, it’s tempting to think businesses are slowing down their ESG efforts. That’s not what we’re seeing.
At Sweep, we work with many companies—across regions and industries—and the momentum is strong. Julien made it clear: sustainability remains a top strategic priority.
“When you’re running a business, you know sustainability is one of the major issues. If you want resilience, if you want to grow, you need solid extra-financial data and a clear environmental strategy.”
Julien Denormandie
CIO, Sweep
Many of our clients aren’t reacting to regulation; they’re preparing for what’s next. Even in markets where policies are softening, the business case for sustainability is only getting stronger. Supply chain volatility, climate risks, and investor expectations don’t pause for politics.
The EU must lead on values, not just cost
The conversation turned to the shifting regulatory landscape in Europe. Julien spoke candidly about the importance of long-term thinking.
“The EU’s competitiveness can’t be based only on cost. It has to be based on value—on transparency, on sustainability, on impact.”
That perspective reflects Sweep’s broader mission. We help companies treat ESG data with the same rigor as financial data—not because they’re told to, but because it’s what their customers, investors, and partners now expect.
Sustainability is now a deciding factor in deals
Jérémie Joos brought in the KPMG perspective—and confirmed what we see across our client base: ESG is shaping outcomes across the business landscape.
“If you’re not strong on sustainability, you won’t win the deal. ESG is now central to how companies compete.”
Jérémie Joos
Partner, Head of ESG Center of Excellence
From RFPs to M&A, ESG credentials are becoming business-critical. Jérémie shared that KPMG clients in private equity and investment banking are already closing—or losing—deals based on sustainability performance.
This is why our strategic partnership with KPMG is so important. Together, we combine powerful technology and deep advisory expertise to help companies not just meet regulations, but lead their industries through transformation.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
