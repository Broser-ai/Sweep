Why leading brands are doubling down on product-level carbon data
From food to furniture, companies across the world are under mounting pressure to show the real impact of the products they sell. And the smartest brands are already ahead of the curve.
Brands like
Caudalie, Swisscom
and
Wickes
are embracing carbon footprinting to gain visibility across complex supply chains, make more informed decisions, and meet the rising bar of sustainability disclosure.
In this guide, you’ll learn what a product carbon footprint really is, why it’s essential for today’s businesses, and how to get started with confidence. Whether you’re reporting to investors, preparing for CSRD, or future-proofing your portfolio, PCFs are your foundation for action.
