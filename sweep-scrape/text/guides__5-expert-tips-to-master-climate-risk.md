Discover 5 expert tips to manage climate risk, strengthen business resilience, and turn environmental challenges into long-term opportunities.
Category
Guides
Last updated
November 13, 2025
Sustainability ROI
Climate risk is already reshaping the way companies operate. As David Carlin, founder of the UNEP FI Risk Centre, explains, “Businesses face these challenges every day. The mistake is to see climate as a future issue. It’s happening now, not in five years, not in 2050.”
According to the
World Economic Forum
, climate-related risks threaten between 5% and 25% of corporate EBITDA. This guide shares practical recommendations from three experts to help organisations strengthen resilience and turn climate challenges into long-term opportunities.
The experts
François Gemenne
, climate expert and lead author of the IPCC’s 6th Assessment Report
David Carlin
, founder of the UNEP FI Risk Centre
Julien Denormandie,
former Minister, Chief Impact Officer at Sweep
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
