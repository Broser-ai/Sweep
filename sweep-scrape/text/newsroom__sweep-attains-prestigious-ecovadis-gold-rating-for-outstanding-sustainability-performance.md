Sweep attains prestigious EcoVadis Gold Rating for outstanding sustainability performance
Last updated
September 24, 2024
Press release
Paris, January 5, 2024
– Sweep, a leading software platform specializing in carbon and ESG data management, is proud to announce its attainment of the prestigious EcoVadis Gold Rating. This recognition underscores the company’s unwavering commitment to sustainability across diverse facets – both within its own practices and in the solutions it delivers for clients.
Evaluated comprehensively on environmental, social, ethical, and responsible procurement criteria, Sweep stands tall in the 93rd percentile globally, showcasing its exceptional dedication to responsible business practices.
EcoVadis, renowned for its rigorous assessments, has recognized Sweep as a top supplier dedicated to helping its clients limit their environmental impact.
The rating also underscores Sweep’s support for social and human rights. The company has demonstrated this through the implementation of diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace policies, coupled with robust initiatives to foster employee well-being.
This EcoVadis Gold Rating aligns closely with Sweep’s overarching mission to provide comprehensive solutions for clients on their decarbonization journey.
“At Sweep, we want to lead by example. Our data-driven platform reflects  the ESG principles that we ourselves also adhere to. As CEO, I’m proud of Sweep’s role in setting a benchmark for responsible business practices, providing not just a platform – but a broader commitment to a more sustainable economy.” –
Rachel Delacour, CEO and co-founder of Sweep
.
Sweep remains steadfast in its dedication to decarbonization, fostering impactful partnerships with businesses across all industries, with financial institutions, and consultancies. It is B Corp certified, and is leading the charge in creating sustainable organizations that prioritize people and the planet – which it calls ‘Forever Companies’.
About Sweep
Sweep is the all-in-one data platform to help organizations manage their carbon and ESG data – from mapping to measuring, reducing to reporting and driving decarbonization action plans. It’s the enterprise-ready solution that helps companies scale climate action across teams, supply chains, and investment portfolios, quickly and cost-effectively.
Cofounded by
Rachel Delacour
, Yannick Chaze and Raphael Gueller, Sweep is B Corp certified and a member of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition, France Invest, and The International Emissions Trading Association. It was selected to join FrenchTech Next 120/40 program and was named “Europe’s fastest growing scale up” by Financial Times’ Sifted.
About EcoVadis
EcoVadis is the world’s most trusted provider of business sustainability ratings, intelligence and collaborative performance improvement tools for global supply chains. Backed by a powerful technology platform and a global team of domain experts, EcoVadis’ easy-to-use and actionable sustainability scorecards provide detailed insight into environmental, social and ethical risks across 200+ purchasing categories and 175+ countries.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
