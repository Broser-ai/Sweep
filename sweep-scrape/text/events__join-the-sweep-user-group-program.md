Climate transformation is a journey, and we understand your challenges when navigating through it.
This is why we're thrilled to introduce the Sweep User Group Program. A dedicated customer program to connect, exchange, and innovate together to fight against climate change. A program designed for you and with you that brings many benefits:
Share Solutions for Common Challenges:
Every customer, whether new or seasoned, encounters challenges. Our program is your platform to share practical solutions and learn from others.
Customer-to-Customer Support:
Need advice? Have insights to share? Our "Customer-to-Customer" initiative makes it seamless for you to both provide and receive support from the community.
Exclusive Access & Feedback Loop:
Be the first to experience new features and have a say in our product roadmap. Your feedback is invaluable in shaping the future of Sweep.
Expand Your Knowledge:
Networking opportunities and industry insights will help you maximize your Sweep investment. Grow your knowledge, save time, and achieve your goals faster.
Ready to Join?
Become a part of a thriving community. Share, learn, and grow with your peers and make a difference.
Agenda:
👋 14h00: Welcome and networking
🎤 14h30: Sweep user group presentation
🎤 14h40: Customer presentation on their emissions' reduction strategy
🎤 15h00: Peer-to-peer discussions on best practices for reducing emissions
🤝 15h30-16h00: Networking break
🎤 16h00: Presentation of the short-term product roadmap
🎤 16h15: Conclusions & networking
