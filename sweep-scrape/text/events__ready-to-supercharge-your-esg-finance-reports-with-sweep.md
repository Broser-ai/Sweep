The paradigm of financial market regulations is rapidly shifting, with a rising emphasis on Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) characteristics. One of those regulations game-changing regulations is the Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation (SFDR) which came into effect in March 2021. SFDR is shaking the foundation of EU-based financial market entities, pushing them to provide detailed disclosures about the ESG attributes of their financial products.
However, these new regulations pose new challenges. From categorizing financial products under SFDR's taxonomy to ensuring consistent and reliable sustainability data, the road to compliance can seem daunting –  that's where Sweep steps in.
We're excited to invite you to our exclusive webinar:
"Do Your PAI Reports on Sweep: Unlocking the Power of ESG Finance"
. With the right tools, tackling SFDR and other regulatory challenges can be seamless and productive. Our enhanced solution,
Sweep for ESG Finance
, is the key to unlock that potential.
In this webinar, we'll cover:
Understanding the objectives of SFDR and its implications
How Sweep for Finance can help you get your carbon and ESG data ready for regulatory compliance
A live demo of Sweep for ESG Finance, showing its practical features and the value it adds to your compliance journey.
Join us in the journey of transforming ESG finance reporting! See you there.
