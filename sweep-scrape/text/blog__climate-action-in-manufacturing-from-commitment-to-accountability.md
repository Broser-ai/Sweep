Climate Action in Manufacturing: From Commitment to Accountability
Category
Blog
Last updated
June 02, 2025
ESG management
As a manufacturing company, you play a pivotal role in shaping the future of sustainability. However,
76% of manufacturers
are only at the planning or foundational stages of their ESG strategy.
The era of making promises has come to an end. A climate emergency is upon us; our clients, employees, and investors demand tangible actions.
Welcome to the age of accountability, where data takes center stage. Here’s where Sweep steps in – we use business intelligence to streamline carbon and ESG management.
Find out how you can turn promises into actions.
Speakers
Renaud Bettin, VP of Climate Action at Sweep
Kayleigh Potter, ESG Strategic Account Manager at Sweep
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
