Last updated

Sustainability ROI
In this guide, you’ll learn how to:
Build a business case
for a sustainability data platform, tailored to key stakeholders.
Quantify costs and ROI
, with clear breakdowns of investment and ongoing expenses.
Use the Verdantix VVD methodology
to highlight financial and strategic benefits.
Address stakeholders’ concerns
and secure buy-in across your organization.
Many sustainability leaders struggle to quantify the return on investment (ROI) of sustainability data management platforms, making it difficult to secure executive buy-in and budget approval. That’s why we’re here to help.
As highlighted in Sweep and Capgemini Invent’s report,
Sustainability in Action: Harnessing the Power of Data to Unlock Sustainable Business Transformation
, sustainability is now a core concern for businesses. Companies are increasingly aware of its potential to drive competitive advantage and corporate responsibility. Yet, despite recognizing the importance of data in driving regulatory compliance, tracking emissions, and optimizing operations, many organizations face significant challenges in managing sustainability data effectively.
From grappling with fragmented data sources to navigating complex reporting requirements, the hurdles can seem overwhelming. This guide will help Sustainability leaders build a compelling business case for investing in a sustainability data management platform by demonstrating its strategic, operational, and financial benefits.
