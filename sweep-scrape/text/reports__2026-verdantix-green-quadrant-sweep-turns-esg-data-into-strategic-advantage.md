Sweep has been named a Leader in the 2026 Verdantix Green Quadrant for Enterprise Carbon Management and Product Carbon Footprinting, recognized among just eight vendors out of 21 for capabilities that turn complex sustainability data into business intelligence.
Verdantix’s independent analysis evaluated major platforms including Cority, IBM, Schneider Electric, Sphera, UL Solutions, Watershed, and Wolters Kluwer, benchmarking solutions built for enterprise complexity and strategic value creation.
