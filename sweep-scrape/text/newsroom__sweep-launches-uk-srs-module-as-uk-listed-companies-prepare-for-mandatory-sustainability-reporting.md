News announcement
Sweep, the sustainability intelligence platform, has announced the general availability of its UK SRS solution This new solution is designed to help companies be ready for the UK Sustainability Reporting Standards ahead of the FY2027 deadline.
Made official by the UK government in February 2026, the UK SRS is the country’s adoption of the ISSB standards (IFRS S1 and IFRS S2), which are quickly becoming the global baseline for sustainability reporting. From the fiscal year 2027, UK-listed companies are required to report. And large private companies are expected to follow 12 to 18 months later.
For most companies, the UK SRS standards are as much a data challenge as a governance one. Sustainability data sits across ERP systems, energy platforms, supplier surveys and spreadsheets. Pulling it together, mapping it to specific disclosures, and keeping a complete audit trail takes real work, on top of narrative answers and internal approvals.
Sweep’s UK SRS solution includes:
Full pre-mapping to every disclosure under UK SRS S1 and UK SRS S2, with guidance notes throughout
Sweepy, Sweep’s AI assistant, which drafts narrative responses and populates data points from information already held in the platform
Approval workflows, audit trails, and supplier interfaces in a single flow – so teams can manage both the data and governance requirements in one place
Cross-framework coverage, so the same dataset covers CSRD, CDP, GRI and investor reporting requirements without duplication
Rachel Delacour, CEO and co-founder at Sweep, said:
“The UK SRS standards are a significant ask for UK-listed companies, and the timeline is tight for any large corporation to get organized for this new report. As an official ISSB licensee, we have deep experience with ISSB-based standards, and that expertise is directly embedded in our UK SRS solution. Sustainability teams get a structured path to compliance, without starting from scratch.”
Sweep has already helped tens of customers report against ISSB-based standards across multiple jurisdictions. Hundreds more global clients rely on Sweep’s solutions to report across the many sustainability frameworks that apply to them. Sweep’s UK SRS solution is built on that same enterprise-ready foundation, developed by a dedicated team of regulatory and sustainability content experts who embed domain knowledge and trusted datasets directly into the platform. Sweepy draws on that domain intelligence to make reporting accessible to teams of all sizes and backgrounds, reducing the time it takes to go from data to disclosure.
