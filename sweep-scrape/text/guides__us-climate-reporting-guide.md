Reporting frameworks
SB 253. New York’s CCDAA. CSRD. UK SRS.
Four major mandatory frameworks covering US companies. Yet most companies still lack the data and systems to report with confidence.
The good news: all four frameworks run on the same core data. Build one strong foundation and you’re covered.
This guide shows you exactly what to do. It brings together insights from
three international experts who helped shape these laws
:
Liane Randolph
, former Chair of CARB; SB 253 and 261 expert
Patrick de Cambourg
, former Chair of EFRAG; CSRD expert
David Carlin
, UK SRS expert.
Each section explains who’s affected, what’s required, and what data is needed.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
