Over 3,000 US companies with significant operations in the EU will be required to comply with the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) over the next few years. Understanding the reporting timelines, the key steps in the disclosure process, and the importance of accurate data management will be crucial for these US companies to meet the reporting requirements in time.
In this webinar, Julien Denormandie, Chief Impact Officer at Sweep and a former French government minister, will discuss the CSRD with Arcadia’s Senior Product Manager, Laura Xu. Don’t miss this opportunity to gain actionable insights from an EU insider on how US companies can successfully navigate the CSRD and stay ahead of the curve.
What will be covered:
Introduction to the CSRD:
Its aims, the metrics it covers, fines for non-compliance
How does it apply to my organization?:
What kinds of companies in the US are likely to be drawn into the EU disclosure scope?
Managing sustainability data:
How to effectively track, disclose, and act on your data using the latest technology
Opportunities for US companies:
How early compliance can offer a competitive advantage
Timelines for US companies:
When do US-based companies need to be disclosing?
