Sweep hits 'Carbon Unicorn' status:
Managing 1 billion+ tonnes of CO2
Category
Company
Last updated
December 17, 2025
News announcement
Sweep, the market-leading carbon and ESG data management platform, has reached a monumental milestone: we now manage over 1 billion tonnes (1 GtCO2) of CO2 across Scopes 1, 2, and 3 thanks to our rapidly growing customer base.
This is more than just a number; it’s a massive step toward accelerating global climate action. To put this in perspective, 1.01 GtCO2 represents nearly 3% of the world’s annual carbon emissions.
This is equivalent to:
The total yearly emissions of Japan
Up to 40% of the European Union’s total footprint
More than three times the total carbon footprint of France
By leveraging Sweep, organizations like L’Oreal, Thales and Crocs are transforming their non-financial data into a business advantage and real climate impact.
Sweep empowers thousands of organizations worldwide to:
Measure & Manage: Streamline sustainability data across entire value chains.
Comply: Easily meet the latest global ESG regulations.
Reduce & Act: Identify carbon hotspots, track progress, and simulate reduction pathways that deliver real results.
This milestone proves the power of having the right tools to tackle sustainability challenges. Sweep isn’t just managing data; we’re enabling the reduction of over a billion tonnes of potential CO2, driving companies toward their transition plans and a net-zero future.
This milestone represents more than a number. It shows what’s possible when organizations have the right tools to tackle sustainability challenges and accelerate climate action.
Rachel Delacour, Sweep’s CEO and co-founder said:
“I couldn’t be prouder. We’re not just tracking data. We’re actively enabling the reduction of emissions at a massive scale, driving the shift toward a net-zero future, with business at the heart of every decision.”
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
