Carbon Accounting
Take control of your emissions data across Scope 1, 2, and 3. Sweep follows GHG Protocol standards and handles corporate carbon footprints, product-level emissions, and financed emissions. All with AI-assistance that works in the most complex organizations.
Learn more
ESG disclosure
Meet every regulatory and stakeholder requirement from one dataset. Built-in support for CSRD, ISSB, GRI, CDP, SB253, and more means you’re ready for current frameworks and future regulations. It’s all built-in: pre-configured indicators, audit trails, and disclosure guidance.
Learn more
Value chain engagement
Engage suppliers, business units, and stakeholders efficiently. Supplier portals and automated data collection tools eliminate manual chasing, while role-based access ensures everyone contributes what they’re responsible for. Scaling your program without scaling your headcount.
Learn more
Audit & assurance
Stay ready for external assurance with investor-grade traceability. Complete data lineage, immutable audit trails, and governance controls mean you can answer any auditor question with confidence—meeting mandatory assurance requirements under CSRD and preparing for future regulations.
Learn more
Business Intelligence
Turn data into insights that drive decisions. Flexible dashboards, scenario modeling, and AI-powered analysis help you identify opportunities, forecast outcomes, and track progress. Sweep makes sustainability data accessible and actionable for finance, procurement, R&D, and operations teams.
