CSRD: from obligation to ESG performance booster
Capgemini and Sweep invite you to ChangeNOW 2024 for a dynamic discussion with Crédit Agricole and L'Oréal on leveraging CSRD for innovative ESG strategies and long-term business transformation.

The adoption of the CSRD is a regulatory turning point for the thousands of companies in Europe who must now get ready for new ESG reporting requirements.
But looking beyond the red tape, there are ways for the CSRD to provide strategic leverage for ESG strategies, to help organisations think outside of classic business models and craft a new relationship with their value chains.
As part of the ChangeNOW 2024 programme, Capgemini and Sweep are delighted to invite you to an action-focussed discussion of these issues and opportunities, with Credit Agricole and L'Oréal.
Together we will address best practice, common difficulties and active solutions, as well as the long-term evolutions in tools, processes, organisation and strategy that are likely to come about in light of the CSRD.
