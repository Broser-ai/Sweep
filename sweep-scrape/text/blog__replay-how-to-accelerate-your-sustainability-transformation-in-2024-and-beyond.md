ESG management
The increasing importance of
ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance)
issues has changed the way businesses operate across industries and regions. However, the ESG landscape is ever evolving and there are new trends coming into force that will shape businesses in 2024 and beyond.
During this Sweep webinar, our CEO, Rachel Delacour will be joined by guest speakers, Thomas Husson, Vice president and Principal Analyst at Forrester and Andrew Winston, co-author of ‘Net Positive: How Courageous Companies Thrive by Giving More than They Take,’ to share a multi-stakeholder perspective on the ways you can accelerate your company’s sustainability transformation.
Our expert panel will discuss:
The key sustainability and ESG trends for 2024 and beyond
How technology (including AI) can help reduce your environmental footprint
Company ESG maturity: Are you a leader or a laggard in sustainability transformation.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
