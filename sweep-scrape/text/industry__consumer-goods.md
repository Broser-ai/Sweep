The consumer goods industry can lead the way in sustainability.
With over $14 trillion in annual consumer spending, the consumer goods industry has immense economic influence. Consumers increasingly seek environmentally and socially responsible products, making ESG strategies not only ethical but also profitable. Sweep provides you with the tools to implement your ESG strategy smoothly and effectively.
Book a demo
Managing a sustainable supply chain
Ensure that every step of your FMCG supply chain is operating sustainably, no matter the size or complexity.
Navigating packaging and waste concerns
Transition to circular economy practices with data-driven insights to reduce waste generated from packaging and unsold products.
Meeting shifting consumer expectations
Align with evolving consumer expectations for FMCG companies to source sustainably and provide transparent ESG reporting.
Products making ESG-related claims averaged 28% cumulative growth over the past five-year period, versus 20% for products that made no such claims.
