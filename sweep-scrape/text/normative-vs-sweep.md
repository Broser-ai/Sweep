Complete ESG integration
Sweep unifies carbon and full ESG data management with extensive native API integrations across ERPs, procurement tools, energy platforms, and data warehouses. This connectivity brings carbon, social, and governance metrics into one auditable system, enabling complete CSRD coverage with consistent data quality across all dimensions.
Normative specializes in carbon footprint calculation supported by Climate Strategists, but offers limited integrations and remains focused primarily on carbon and climate metrics rather than comprehensive ESG reporting.
Regulatory coverage
Sweep handles multi-framework compliance in one platform: CSRD (all ESRs), California SB 253/SB 261, CDP, GRI, ISSB, TCFD, and SFDR, with audit-ready outputs across E, S, and G dimensions.
Normative provides compliance-ready reporting for SECR, CSRD (climate focus), and GHG Protocol-aligned disclosures, best suited for carbon-specific regulatory requirements.
Collaboration at scale
Sweep enables real-time workflows across internal teams, suppliers, and portfolio companies with surveys, shared dashboards, and automated data collection. Manage accountability across your entire value chain in one place.
Normative offers automated supplier data collection focused on carbon emissions with real-time visualization, but broader cross-functional collaboration and workflow automation are more limited.
Assurance readiness
Sweep provides an enterprise SaaS platform with built-in workflows, role-based access, and AI automation that scales across thousands of users. Your team owns the process with full platform control.
Normative combines software with GHG Protocol-certified Climate Strategists who provide tailored advice and a 100% SBTi submission success rate, an expert-led approach that works well for carbon-focused programs but requires more consultancy involvement.
AI-powered Scalability
Sweep embeds AI across the platform, from data mapping and quality checks to disclosure writing and predictive analytics. AI agents handle complex multi-entity data at scale while maintaining accuracy.
Normative uses AI-powered matching to connect business activities with emission factors, streamlining carbon calculations, focused on emissions accuracy rather than enterprise workflow automation.

FAQs: Normative vs Sweep
What’s the difference between Normative and Sweep when it comes to carbon accounting?
Normative is a carbon accounting software solution focused on precise carbon footprint measurement, GHG Protocol–aligned calculations, and expert-guided climate support. It’s built primarily for organisations that want a structured approach to carbon emissions tracking and reduction. Integrating such software solutions can help a business make more efficient and strategic decisions, enhancing overall operations and competitive advantage.
Normative’s key features include 349,000 emission factors, activity-based matching, and advisory services, making it a strong choice for any company, regardless of size or industry, with specific needs around carbon-focused reporting.
Sweep, on the other hand, is a comprehensive tool and sustainability management platform designed for enterprises that need more than carbon accounting alone. As one of the best Normative alternatives, Sweep combines carbon management, ESG data collection (including energy consumption), value-chain collaboration, and multi-framework sustainability reporting, all in an AI powered platform built for scalability.
It helps businesses measure and manage their total environmental impact, unify complex data, integrate with existing systems, and generate audit-ready disclosures that meet rising regulatory requirements such as CSRD, CDP, GRI, ISSB, and SB 253.
For organisations evaluating alternatives to Normative, Sweep stands out as the more comprehensive solution with broader features, deeper collaboration tools, and enterprise-grade automation across various industries and sectors.
Which platform is better for CSRD compliance?
Sweep is considered one of the best alternatives for full CSRD alignment because it covers all European Sustainability Reporting Standards, not just climate. The platform supports double materiality, qualitative and quantitative data collection across environmental, social, and governance topics, and automatically maps data to ESRS requirements.
Its generative AI agents accelerate disclosure writing, and its intuitive design ensures teams can create audit-ready disclosures that help organizations meet regulatory requirements with high efficiency. Sweep also supports finance teams in managing ESG-related financial data and reporting, and using the platform can help reduce costs associated with compliance and sustainability initiatives.
Normative supports CSRD reporting with a focus on ESRS E1 (climate). Its expert strategists guide companies through GHG calculations and climate-related compliance needs. This makes it useful for companies whose regulatory footprint is primarily about emissions and carbon footprint analysis.
For organisations managing broader ESG obligations or navigating complex corporate structures, Sweep offers the more complete, future-proof solution.
How do the two platforms handle supply chain engagement?
Normative provides supplier questionnaires and automated data workflows centered on carbon footprint insights to help companies track and improve Scope 3 emissions. Its tools help organisations integrate primary data sources from the supply chain into their carbon calculations, while also enabling monitoring of environmental impact data.
Sweep expands this significantly with a multi-tier collaboration model that supports ESG and carbon programs simultaneously. Suppliers and value-chain partners can join via a free tier, submit data, set their own targets, track improvement, and engage across broader sustainability topics, not just carbon.
The platform also enables organizations to easily report sustainability data to stakeholders. Sweep’s visibility features, including the “carbon cockpit,” provide actionable insights that help businesses, financial institutions, and global enterprises make data-driven decisions and reduce emissions across their network. In addition to collaboration, Sweep helps improve engagement with customers by providing tailored sustainability solutions.
For organisations looking for deeper collaboration, automation, and ESG-wide tracking, Sweep is the stronger choice among Normative competitors.
What about expert support and guidance?
Normative differentiates itself with GHG Protocol-certified Climate Strategists who guide businesses through carbon calculations, SBTi submissions, and reduction roadmaps. This advisory-heavy approach works well for teams seeking ongoing hands-on support.
Sweep provides a hybrid support model combining in-platform guidance, e-learning through Sweep School, customer success teams, and a network of expert partners. Rather than relying on continuous advisory services, Sweep empowers internal teams to build knowledge and control processes through powerful, easy-to-use tools, workflows, and automation, including creating sustainability objectives and targets within the platform. The platform’s user-centric design enables users to collaborate effectively and manage compliance processes, helping companies accelerate internal capability building and streamline processes at scale.
Which platform is right for my organisation?
Choose Normative if you:
Are primarily focused on carbon accounting, climate disclosure, and carbon footprint reporting
Want hands-on advisory support from Climate Strategists
Need strong SBTi guidance or help setting climate targets
Have simpler regulatory requirements centered on carbon (SECR, GHG Protocol)
Choose Sweep if you:
Want one of the best Normative alternatives with broader, integrated sustainability management capabilities
Need to manage ESG performance across environmental, social, and governance topics
Require multi-framework reporting (CSRD all ESRS, CDP, GRI, SB 253, ISSB)
Operate in various industries with complex structures, multiple entities, and extensive value chains
Need an ai powered platform that unifies complex data and supports enterprise workflows
Want powerful collaboration tools for suppliers, employees, partners, and internal teams
Prefer a self-service, scalable platform with strong integrations and automation
Want a future-ready solution that helps your organisation evaluate performance, track progress, monitor emissions, and continuously improve
Sweep’s comprehensive features, modern architecture, and broader ESG coverage make it the stronger long-term choice for organizations looking to build mature, scalable, enterprise-wide sustainability programs.
