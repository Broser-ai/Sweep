Everyone talks about sustainability, CO2 emissions, and ESG these days, but
where do you start when it comes to turning your business strategy toward a sustainable transformation?
The science is crystal clear: the world must reduce its greenhouse gas emissions by
43% by 2030
to limit global warming to
1.5°C above
pre-industrial levels.
With that said, taking action is no longer an option, but a necessity for companies wishing to have an environmentally virtuous approach.
Fortunately, there are solutions out there, and
Sweep
is among the best. It allows you to measure, understand your impact, and take action for potential reductions within your organisation. Additional to PALO IT's expertise, approach, and newly-minted
Impact Tracker tool
,
PALO IT can help you kickstart your journey toward sustainability by combining both our expertise.
In this session, you'll learn:
How to kickstart your sustainability transformation,
Manage data integration and processing within solutions like Sweep,
Steer your company toward a greener future!
Who should attend this session:
The
RSE and ESG managers
who are considering carbon accounting tools
The
Chief Financial Officers (CFOs)
who are required to implement a regulatory reporting of their emissions
The
operational teams and Human Resources
who want to quantify and engage their staff in their sustainable transformation.
Technical professionals
who need to source data and integrate it into carbon accounting tools.
The
Marketing and Communication managers
who wish to include quantifiable data in their company's communications.
The
executives and founders
of companies who want to inform themselves or integrate the measurement of their carbon footprint into their strategy.
