5 Steps to smarter, AI-augmented sustainability
Discover 5 steps to smarter, AI-powered sustainability. Streamline data, cut admin, boost accuracy, and turn ESG reporting into real business impact.
Category
Blog
Last updated
December 08, 2025
Sustainability ROI
Sustainability teams are capable of incredible impact, but only when the data behind them works.
With the right structure, automation, and clarity, sustainability data transforms from a bottleneck into a catalyst: reporting becomes smoother, decarbonization becomes targeted and achievable, and strategic decisions become stronger.
When your data is reliable and accessible, the entire organization can move faster, collaborate better, and embed sustainability at the heart of business performance.
Here’s how to make this happen in 5 steps.
1. Efficiency: Reduce admin and move to action
The most effective sustainability teams today aren’t drowning in spreadsheets, they’re building intelligence into their data from the start. With one platform, smart automation, and a single consistent foundation, messy multi-sheet workflows become clean, connected, and auditable.
The result is faster reporting, sharper insights, and the ability to align ESG data with core business intelligence, giving teams a real competitive edge in a world of rising economic and environmental pressure.
If you are not strong on sustainability, you will not win the deal. ESG is now central to how companies compete.
Jérémie Joos
Partner and ESG Center of Excellence Lead
How Sweep helps
Sweep consolidates all your climate and ESG data into one system. Teams eliminate hours of manual admin each week and recover the time needed to drive decarbonization, supplier engagement and strategic planning.
Sweep was the best tool on the market for putting all of our data into a single source of truth and visualizing where carbon emissions were highest.
Mélissa Saint-Fort
ESG Director
2. Build precision with AI
On the topic of applied AI, Verdantix is clear: the most advanced sustainability teams will be those who use agentic AI to simplify complex technical tasks. The new frontier is not more tools, but
AI integrations that remove the manual tasks of carbon accounting, reporting and compliance, while improving accuracy.
Sustainability workflows that once required expert judgment “can now be accomplished through natural language queries,” making high quality data work accessible to teams across the organization.
Accuracy matters because sustainability is now a risk discipline which impacts your bottom line. And risk only moves at the speed of trusted data.
Climate risk is financial risk. We can no longer act as though businesses are operating in a climate-stable world, because we are not in that world.
Kate Gordon
CEO, California Forward, former advisor to Senator Newsome
How Sweep helps
Sweep’s AI agents are trained on sustainability expertise, not generic text. They automatically map datasets, flags anomalies, assigns emission factors and drafts disclosure responses with full traceability and human in the loop. The result is audit-ready accuracy without the bottlenecks.
3. Spend less, understand more
Budgets are tightening while expectations expand. Sustainability teams cannot simply hire their way to maturity. The only path forward is to remove the hidden costs that absorb time and hinder performance.
Fragmented data creates duplication. Manual data cleanup leads to errors. When data becomes structured, automated and governed, these costs disappear.
And the financial upside becomes clear: better energy insights, cleaner supply chain data, faster audits and fewer surprises.
Reacting costs more than planning – and investors are already stress-testing for climate and social risks.
Cara Williams
Global Head of Climate, Mercer
How Sweep helps
Sweep eliminates the most expensive parts of sustainability programs. Automated data ingestion, anomaly detection, AI-assisted mapping, and repeatable workflows reduce internal sustainability data management costs by up to
48 percent
(Verdantix).
4. Boost audit confidence
Boards and regulators want more than carbon totals. They want evidence. They want traceability. They want assurance that every number is backed by a clear chain of custody.
This is only possible when data is fully documented: where it came from, how it was transformed, which emission factor was used, and why. The same is true for AI. When teams have transparency over every AI assisted step, they can trust the output and stand behind it.
With the right data, you can prove impact in numbers, and that is when leadership really pays attention.
Rachel Delacour
CEO
How Sweep helps
Sweep gives every dataset an evidence trail. AI contributions are predictable, logged, and reviewable. Boundaries, factors and assumptions are documented. This reduces audit risk, simplifies assurance and gives leadership the clarity needed to take action.
5. Transform your business through data
The real value of sustainability data is not in the report at year end. It is in the decisions made every week.
When organizations can finally see their hotspots, dependencies, risks and opportunities with precision, they start redesigning how the business operates: new supplier strategies, more efficient logistics, resilient assets, lower cost operations and better long term planning.
The companies doing this now will shape the next decade of competition.
We want our product development, sourcing, and commercial teams to go into Sweep, get the carbon data they need, and make decisions from the get-go.
Alisa Heimann
Group Sustainability Manager
How Sweep helps
Sweep provides the data infrastructure for true business transformation. It connects teams, surfaces insights, guides operational choices and ensures that sustainability goals turn into measurable action across the company.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
