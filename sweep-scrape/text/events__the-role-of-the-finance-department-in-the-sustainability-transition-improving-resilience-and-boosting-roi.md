## Content


EVENT
The role of the finance department in the sustainability transition
Join experts from Chanel, Sweep, and BearingPoint as they explore how effectively integrating sustainability into financial planning can mitigate risk and support the long term-growth of a company.
Location
Online
Date
Apr 02, 2025
Time
2:00 PM
Language
English
Category
Webinar
Last updated
November 26, 2025
A business’s finance department is crucial in steering the sustainability transition by not only financing green initiatives but also identifying new growth opportunities that ensure long-term resilience. As is widely acknowledged, CFOs and financial directors must balance profit maximization with careful consideration of both their organization’s impact on the environment, and the environment’s impact on the organization.,
This webinar will explore how effectively integrating sustainability into financial planning, through the right strategies and technological tools, can mitigate risk and support the long term-growth of a company.
Key Takeaways:
Understanding the finance department’s role:
Explore how finance teams can drive the sustainability agenda by financing key initiatives that deliver both environmental impact and financial returns.
Maximizing ROI:
Learn how sustainable investments contribute to business development, foster innovation, and enhance competitiveness by keeping you ahead of regulatory requirements and on track with evolving consumer demands.
Building Resilience:
Discover how integrating sustainable practices can mitigate risks, making your orgainzation more resilient in the face of environmental and regulatory challenges.
Leveraging Technology:
Delve into the importance of implementing the right technological tools to manage sustainability data, streamline reporting, and support informed decision-making for long-term success.
