Last updated

CSRD
On February 26, 2025, the European Commission unveiled the
Omnibus Proposal
, aiming to simplify sustainability reporting by adjusting the thresholds for the
Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD)
.
These changes could mean that some companies no longer fall within its scope—but does that mean sustainability disclosures should take a back seat?
Take this
2-minute questionnaire
to find out if your company is still affected and why reporting remains a strategic advantage.
What’s next?
If your company is still impacted by the
CSRD
, get ahead of your data compliance obligations today—
Sweep
can help you achieve compliance in weeks, not months. Find out more
here
.
If CSRD reporting is no longer mandatory for your company, you can still voluntarily disclose your sustainability impact. Staying transparent with your environmental and social efforts will enhance your resilience, boost your competitive edge, and position you for long-term success. Find out more
here
.
