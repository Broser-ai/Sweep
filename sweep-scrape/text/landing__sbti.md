What is the SBTi Net Zero Standard?
The SBTi’s Corporate Net Zero Standard sets science-based rules for companies to reach net zero and keep warming below 1.5°C. To comply, companies must:
– Cut emissions fast – halve them by 2030.
– Go long-term – slash at least 90% by 2050.
– Neutralise what’s left – use permanent carbon removal for any residual emissions.
– Go beyond the value chain – support climate action outside your direct operations.
It’s the world’s leading framework for credible, science-aligned net zero strategies.
Key principles of science-based target
Science-based targets are built on four core principles:
– Full emissions coverage – include Scope 1, Scope 2, and (usually) Scope 3.
– Real cuts, not offsets – focus on absolute emissions reductions.
– Aligned with Net Zero Standard – set near- and long-term goals.
– Annual accountability – report progress every year.
How Sweep can help with SBTi?
With Sweep, you can:
– Measure with precision – run a full carbon footprint assessment across Scope 1, 2, and 3.
– See your value chain clearly – get real-time insights into supplier performance and progress toward your targets.
– Stay ahead of regulations – align with CSRD and other ESG requirements fast.
– Report with confidence – verify your sustainability data with third-party assurance before you share it.
