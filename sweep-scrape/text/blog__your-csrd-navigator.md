Your first step to CSRD compliance
If you don’t know where to start with CSRD reporting, you’re not alone. The process can be overwhelming, especially when you hear that you need to collect more than 1,200 data points. But you’ll be relieved to hear that the number that apply to your business is actually much lower.
*Please note that these results are based on the current ESRS standards and may be subject to change following the
Omnibus
vote. We will make updates as new information becomes available.
Methodology
We analyzed hundreds of companies in different sectors to provide you with precise information on the number of data points you need to collect, based on your industry and company size. You’ll find these terms on the navigator:
Mandatory data point
: data point that you need to collect now for your reporting
Voluntary data point
: data point that you can choose to include in your reporting
Voluntary data point in the first year
: data point that you can choose to include in your reporting, and that will become mandatory for the following reporting period.
Qualitative indicator:
you’ll provide qualitative data (text) for this type of indicator.
Quantitative indicator:
you’ll provide quantitative data (number) for this type of indicator
What’s next?
Now that you know the number of data points that you need to report on, you’ll need to put your indicator list together, and start collecting your data. This is where Sweep comes in.

Efficiently collect data from all your stakeholders
Easily compile, export and submit your report in the ESAP platform.
