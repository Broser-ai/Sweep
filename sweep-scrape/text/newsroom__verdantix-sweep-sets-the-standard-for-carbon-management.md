Verdantix: Sweep Sets the Standard for Carbon Management
Category

Last updated

News announcement
Carbon Management
Sweep, the sustainability data management platform, has been recognized in the latest  “Smart Innovators: Carbon Management Software 2025” report by Verdantix, an independent research and advisory firm.
This latest accolade highlights Sweep’s ongoing commitment to helping companies worldwide accelerate their decarbonization journey and move from reporting to real impact.
Verdantix recognized Sweep for its exceptional, market-leading capabilities in two critical building blocks for organizations committed to making tangible progress on decarbonization:
Target Management – how the software enables users to set and track short-
and long-term targets, objectives, KPIs for decarbonization at a corporate, division and facility-level
AI-powered query assistance – how the software uses AI to provide insight to user queries about carbon data, internal policies, best practices or performance benchmarks
The platform also received high marks for its scenario planning, project management, abatement, cost analysis, and robust data management features, making it a comprehensive solution for today’s complex sustainability landscape.
Sweep empowers organizations to easily understand and act on all extra-financial data across their business and value chain. As disclosure requirements grow ever more complex, Sweep enables businesses to not only meet compliance needs but also turn sustainability into a driver of growth.
Thousands of businesses rely on Sweep to support decarbonization initiatives, leverage sustainability programs for innovation, and drive meaningful carbon reduction action throughout supply chains.
By connecting sustainability, finance, procurement, and operations teams on a single collaborative platform, Sweep ensures that everyone in the organization pulls in the same direction – turning ambition into achievement.
“Our mission is to help businesses close the gap between sustainability ambition and measurable results,”
said Rachel Delacour, CEO and Co-founder of Sweep.
“This most recent recognition from Verdantix further validates our belief that technology, data, and collaboration are the keys to unlocking progress at scale. Sweep is built to empower organizations to deliver results, so they can move from reporting and compliance to creating lasting business value.”
With its focus on actionable intelligence, decision support and cross-functional collaboration, Sweep continues to set the standard for carbon management and sustainability data solutions. As the pressure to deliver on environmental commitments intensifies worldwide, Sweep stands ready to help organizations transform sustainability from a box-ticking exercise into a catalyst for innovation and growth.

About Sweep
Sweep is the sustainability data management platform. Its market-leading, AI-powered software helps organizations understand all extra-financial data across their business and value chain to manage increasing disclosure requirements and take action to meet sustainable business goals. Sweep has repeatedly been recognized for its market-leading capabilities by organizations including Verdantix, IDC MarketScape and MEDEF.
Co-founded by Rachel Delacour, Yannick Chaze and Raphael Guller, Sweep partners with enterprise, midmarket and financial institutions across the world, with customers including L’Oreal, QVC, and Lacoste.
Sweep is B Corp certified and a member of the World Bank’s Carbon Pricing Leadership Coalition, France Invest and The International Emissions Trading Association.
About Verdantix
Verdantix is an independent research and advisory firm that serves a global client base consisting of the world’s most innovative corporations, technology and services vendors, and investors. Our insights and analysis form a foundation of the most granular data available in the marketplaces we serve. This allows us to make highly accurate far-reaching forecasts and big-picture predictions that business leaders depend on when they are setting out to reach their most important goals.
