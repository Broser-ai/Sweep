## Content


EVENT
From distributor to supplier (Sweep x Casino)
We explore how SaaS technology revolutionizes distribution to tackle challenges of decarbonizing the retail value chain.
Location
Paris
Date
Mar 14, 2024
Time
7:30 AM
Language
French
Category
Sweep event
Last updated
November 04, 2024
Supply Chain
By bringing goods and services to market, distribution plays a key role in decarbonizing the retail value chain. In this context, Saas technology offers a powerful and effective framework for tackling the challenges of an industry under pressure from consumer behavior, ever-increasing regulation and dependence on suppliers.
Over breakfast, Sweep takes you on a journey to the heart of the distributor-supplier relationship, in the light of digital solutions. On the menu: coffee, croissant and more:
Distributors' climate management needs and Sweep's offer
A supplier testimonial on distributor relationships
Presentation of Open climate, a data pool for distributors and suppliers
