SB 253 reporting deadlines are approaching fast. Understanding what regulators expect is essential to achieve assurance first time.
Watch this on-demand webinar with
Liane Randolph, former Chair of the California Air Resources Board (CARB)
, for a practical insights on how companies should structure emissions data and prepare for disclosure.
We’ll cover:
What SB 253 requires from a data and reporting standpoint
How to approach Scope 3 in the early years
What regulators expect to see in practice
How SB 253, New York’s CCDAA and other frameworks can be supported by a single reporting foundation
Watch on-demand now… 👇
