The alliance between Sweep and JCDecaux praised by the David avec Goliath Prize
Last updated
September 24, 2024
News announcement
The joint collaboration between Sweep and JCDecaux has been recognized by the prestigious David avec Goliath Prize, celebrating successful partnerships between a startup and a corporation.
Sweep is delighted to share that its climate work with
JCDecaux
, the global leader in outdoor advertising, was selected for
David avec Goliath Prize
, which recognizes fruitful alliances between large and young companies.
Led by
impact fund Raise
and consulting firm
Bain & Company
, the David avec Goliath Prize aims to build bridges between startups and corporate organizations by showcasing successful collaborations and sharing good practices.
JCDecaux Group
is the largest outdoor advertising corporation in the world. It’s committed to developing innovative solutions in response to environmental challenges and integrating sustainability into the fabric of our products. In 2014, the
Sustainable Development Strategy
was implemented across the JCDecaux Group.
Learn how Sweep can help large corporations
reduce their climate impact
.
Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
See how we can help you on your sustainability journey
