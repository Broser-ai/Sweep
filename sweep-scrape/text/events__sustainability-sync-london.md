Celebrate the successes of 2024 with us at our end-of-year Sustainability Sync in London!
It’s the perfect chance to get together with sustainability-minded professionals from across our network, celebrate 2024, share your successes and learnings, and look ahead to what the next year has in store.
You can expect engaging conversations, delicious refreshments, and the chance to connect with like-minded experts as we ring in the festive season
.

17:30: Doors open
