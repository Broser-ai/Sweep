Meet The Sweepers
Partner
Join other Sweep users for an afternoon of hands-on workshops and peer exchange, designed to help you get more from your ESG reporting.
We’ll focus on disclosures in 2026 and what happens next—exploring how to manage the reporting process and use your data to inform decisions throughout the year.
What to expect:
Work on your real challenges:
Share your progress, your blockers, and your ideas with other users and our experts.
Prepare for what’s next:
Gain perspective on evolving ESG regulations and what it means for you.
Shape the product:
Share your needs with our product team and help decide our direction.
