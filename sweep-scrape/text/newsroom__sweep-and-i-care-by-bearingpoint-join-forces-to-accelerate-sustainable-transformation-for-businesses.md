News announcement
With its unique positioning as a carbon and ESG data management platform, Sweep has become a go-to solution to support changemakers on their sustainability journey. By collecting activity data from their stakeholders, Sweep’s customers gain a precise and auditable view of their environmental footprint. They can then drive their sustainability performance using advanced analytics and management tools.
Over the course of our projects, we have come to believe that the customers who get the most out of Sweep are those who have truly taken ownership of environmental topics, starting with carbon footprint measurement and CSRD reporting. To help them deepen their methodologies, build their decarbonization pathways, or steer their transition plans, we work with I Care by BearingPoint, a leader in sustainable transformation.
I Care by BearingPoint combines deep environmental expertise with the innovation, transformation, and technology strengths of a consulting firm. Through a catalog of customizable offerings, our partner can support you across all your sustainability needs, helping you define or improve your carbon accounting methodology in line with relevant standards (GHG Protocol, regulatory BEGES, Bilan Carbone®, etc.). They review your corporate carbon footprint ahead of an audit and calculate your product carbon footprints (PCF), both integrated directly into Sweep.
They also support you in assessing your climate risks, conducting a double materiality assessment, and defining a realistic, controlled decarbonization pathway aligned with SBTi requirements.
I Care leverages its experience implementing Sweep for 25 clients across 8 countries. This expertise enables full alignment between methodology and platform configuration.
Both Sweep and BearingPoint have achieved B Corp certification at the organizational level, demonstrating a shared, structured, and voluntary commitment to sustainable transformation.

About BearingPoint
BearingPoint is an independent management and technology consulting firm with European roots and an international reach.
We help businesses transform by combining deep sector expertise with strong capabilities in strategy, operations, and technology. Our teams’ expertise in SAP and Microsoft transformations, paired with sustained investment in artificial intelligence and results-driven solutions, enables us to develop tailored, innovative approaches that create lasting value.
In addition to its core consulting activities, BearingPoint operates two joint ventures. Arcwide, a partnership with IFS, specializes in enterprise transformations powered by IFS technologies. BearingPoint North America, a joint venture with ABeam Consulting, is dedicated to consulting excellence and SAP-driven transformations.
BearingPoint serves some of the world’s largest public and private organizations. Together with its strategic partner ABeam Consulting, the firm brings together more than 15,000 professionals, including over 1,000 in France, and supports clients in more than 70 countries by delivering integrated operational transformations, enhancing performance, and generating lasting impact.
BearingPoint is featured among TIME World’s Best Companies and Forbes World’s Best Employers. The firm has also been B Corp certified since 2024, affirming its commitment to a responsible and sustainable business model for organizations, its people, and society at large.
