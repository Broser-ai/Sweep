Sweep launches Sweep School, a climate training platform
Last updated
December 01, 2025
News announcement
Embedded in Sweep’s carbon management platform, Sweep School empowers users to learn and implement decarbonization best practices within their organizations.
Sweep School is a self-paced training program to help platform users take the lead on their company’s climate journey using Sweep. It includes step-by-step guides, industry use cases, and education materials on carbon management and corporate sustainability.
As part of its commitment to accelerating global decarbonization, Sweep aims to empower business professionals to drive climate action in their companies. With Sweep School, they can easily get to grips with collecting, measuring and acting on their carbon data, involving team members and partners. Modules cover a range of key topics, such as how to get started with carbon measurement and communicate corporate climate impact.
Designed by Sweep’s carbon experts, Sweep School also provides templates to help customers wherever they are in their climate journeys – from collecting the carbon data of manufacturing power plants to mapping products’ emission sources in the cosmetics supply chain.
“With Sweep School, I got access to concrete examples and best practices on carbon management, which allowed me to tailor our use of Sweep to our company’s structure and decarbonization goals. It’s super convenient to have everything in one place,” says Rachael Spelman, Sustainability intern team at Prose.
The sustainable beauty brand has been using Sweep for over a year to measure the carbon emissions per product and find alternatives to design low carbon products.
As Sweep is gaining momentum in the finance industry, its finance expert team led by
Marie-Anne Vincent
, Sweep’s VP of Climate Finance, is now working on a new module on decarbonizing financed emissions.
