For its first edition, the Private Equity France event, organized by L'Agefi and Private Equity Magazine, analyzes the changes that are shaping the private equity industry in France.
We are pleased to inform you that we will be participating as a sponsor in this edition, which will focus on the theme:
What drivers of performance in 2023?
Our VP of Climate Finance, Marie-Anne Vincent, will speak at the closing conference: Integrating ESG… but to what degree? How can private equity funds evolve to better meet the requirements of institutional investors? What about labels and certifications?
