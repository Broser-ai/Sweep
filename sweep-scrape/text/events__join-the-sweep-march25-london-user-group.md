Meet The Sweepers
Partner
The Sweep User Group Program is a customer-driven initiative. Its purpose is to encourage connection, sharing and innovation to help you improve your sustainability performance.
Share solutions to common challenges:
Every customer faces challenges. Use our program as a platform to share practical solutions and learn from others.
Exclusive access & feedback loop:
Be the first to experience new features and contribute to our product roadmap. Your feedback will help shape the future of Sweep.
Expand your knowledge:
Networking opportunities and industry insights can help you get the most out of your Sweep investment. Expand your knowledge, save time, and achieve your goals faster.
Join us March 25 as we explore the question:
How to master data governance to better manage your sustainable development programme?
Programme:
👋 12:00: Welcome and networking

🤝 15h00-15h30: Break and networking
🎤 15h30-16h30: Presentation of the product roadmap + Q&A
🤝 16h30: Conclusions and networking
Ready to Join?
Join us for this upcoming event in London. Share, learn, and grow with your peers to create meaningful impact.
