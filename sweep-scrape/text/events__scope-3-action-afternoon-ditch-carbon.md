Sweep will be taking part in the
Scope 3 Action Afternoon
, an afternoon of candid conversation, real-world stories, and practical insights into some of the most pressing challenges in carbon management today.
Organised by DitchCarbon, this in-person event brings together leading voices in sustainability for two panel sessions designed to cut through the noise and focus on what actually drives climate action.
Rachel Delacour, CEO and co-founder of Sweep
, will be joining the panel for
“Where integration can really make impact”
, alongside representatives from Salesforce, SAP and others. Expect honest discussion, customer stories, and real results from organisations putting their ESG stack to work.
