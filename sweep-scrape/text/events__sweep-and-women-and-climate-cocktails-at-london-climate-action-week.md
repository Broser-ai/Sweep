[REGISTRATIONS NOW CLOSED]
Sweep and Women and Climate are co-hosting a cocktail networking event during London Climate Action Week.
This event will provide an opportunity to celebrate the work of women in climate and sustainability, share thoughts, challenges and ideas around key issues for our sector, and network with leaders and rising stars.
Women and Climate founder Michelle Li will guide us through an inspiring evening of discussions and networking, including:
Agenda:
18:00 – Networking and arrivals
18:40 – Welcome | Michelle Li, Founder, Women and Climate
18:45 – Fireside Chat | Michelle Li with Rachel Delacour, CEO and Co-founder, Sweep
Sweep’s CEO and co-founder Rachel Delacour will be sharing insights into how she built on her Business Intelligence background, to become a leading sustainability entrepreneur.
18:55 – C-Suites and Board Rooms: the Role of Data | Panel discussion
What is the role of data when it comes to the C-Suite and boardroom? How is data used today and how will it change with more stringent regulations?
Anna Richardson – Former Sustainability Program Director at GSK
Eve Joseph – Head of Social Impact at British Triathlon
Manisha Shastri – VP, Net Zero Strategy at HSBC
19:20 – Rising Stars in Sustainability | Panel discussion
Highlighting rising stars in the sustainability space and learning about their work, journey, and learnings.
Kayleigh Potter, Sweep Account Executive and London Women And Climate City Lead | Moderator
Felicity Box, Solutions Engineer, Sweep
Sabrina Pieraccioli, Partnerships Manager, Ocean Bottle
Emilie Huyghues Despointes, ESG Officer, MV Credit
19:35 – Networking, cocktails, nibbles
Rachel Delacour
Cofounder and CEO
Michelle  Li
Founder
