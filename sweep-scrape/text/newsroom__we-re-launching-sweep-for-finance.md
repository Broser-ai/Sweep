We’re launching Sweep for Finance
Last updated
June 04, 2025
News announcement
We’re taking another step to accelerate global decarbonization with Sweep for Finance, our first industry-focused solution to empower financial institutions to decarbonize across investments.
When I first met Marie-Anne Vincent at COP26, our conversation led to the drawing of the decarbonization process of an investment firm on a napkin. That was the start of Sweep for Finance.
On this napkin, there was a powerful ecosystem driving the world’s economy – with investors at its heart. They navigate across various networks to source top founders, support portfolio companies, and connect with LPs to back the next generation of businesses.
Because all these actors directly or indirectly feed into each other’s carbon footprint, they can trigger domino effects to enable whole value chains and industries to decarbonize.
With that in mind, we built Sweep for Finance to facilitate the exchange of carbon data by connecting investors to portfolio companies – delivering a complete, real-time, and auditable overview of their financed emissions.
Most importantly, financial institutions can now take action with their investees – whether it’s a US startup that has yet to start measuring emissions or a UK corporation looking to get accurate data to comply with new climate disclosure requirements. This tailored network approach is the reason why leading organizations, such as Coatue, Mirova, and
2050
, chose Sweep for Finance to reach their climate targets.
From napkin to software, we’re proud to unveil the most powerful solution for collaborating at every step of a climate journey – empowering investors and their portfolio companies to join forces on scaling climate action globally.
Get your financed emissions on-track
Rachel Delacour
