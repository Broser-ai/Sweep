Sweep named a 2025 Smart Innovator by Verdantix for carbon management, excelling in target tracking, AI insights, and helping businesses drive real impact.
Category

Last updated

Carbon accounting
Sweep, the sustainability data management platform, has been recognized as a
Smart Innovator in Carbon Management Software 2025
by Verdantix, the independent research and advisory firm.
This recognition highlights our ongoing commitment to helping companies worldwide move beyond reporting and accelerate real decarbonization impact.
Sweep excels in carbon management capabilities
In its report, Verdantix identified two areas where Sweep leads the market:
Target management
– empowering organizations to set and track short- and long-term decarbonization targets, KPIs, and objectives at every level: corporate, division, and facility.
AI-powered query assistance
– providing instant insights to user queries about carbon data, policies, benchmarks, and best practices.
These innovations help companies translate climate ambition into measurable outcomes, while making sustainability management more accessible across the business.
Comprehensive features for complex challenges
Verdantix also rated Sweep highly for additional capabilities that are critical in today’s sustainability landscape:
Scenario planning and abatement
–
modeling pathways to net zero and identifying the most effective actions.
Project and cost management
– linking climate initiatives to financial planning and ROI.
Robust data management
– ensuring accuracy, transparency, and auditability across value chains.
Together, these features make Sweep a comprehensive solution for businesses navigating disclosure rules and rising stakeholder expectations.
A platform built for collaboration
At Sweep, we know sustainability can’t be solved in silos. Thousands of businesses already use our platform to:
Manage emissions across their value chain
Engage finance, procurement, operations, and sustainability teams in one place
Turn compliance into a driver of innovation and growth
As our CEO and co-founder Rachel Delacour explains:
“Our mission is to help businesses close the gap between sustainability ambition and measurable results. This recognition from Verdantix validates our belief that technology, data, and collaboration are the keys to unlocking progress at scale.”
Meeting the moment
As disclosure requirements like CSRD and ISSB evolve, companies need technology that goes beyond compliance to deliver actionable intelligence and business value. Sweep is built to help organizations meet that challenge – turning sustainability into a catalyst for long-term resilience and growth.
