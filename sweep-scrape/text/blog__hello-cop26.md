ESG management
The business of climate – and carbon management – can be overwhelming. Let’s support each other on our reduction journeys.
If there’s one thing that we’ve learned since we started digging into carbon data, it’s that it all comes down to collaboration. With your team. Your investors. Your supply chain partners. The world.
And what better way to stir up collective climate effort than during COP26?
1. Get your finances in order
We are at COP26 to show how our tool can help finance organisations measure their carbon footprint effectively, as part of the Tech For Our Planet challenge, organised by the UK Cabinet Office and Public. We’ve partnered with French investment bank
Bpifrance
and its two portfolio companies
Orange
and
Openclassrooms
to demonstrate how financial organisations can reduce emissions collaboratively across company borders.
📽 Watch our pitch
Our big presentation (yes, big, on the IMAX screen) is on 11 Nov from 6 to 8pm. You can tune in
here
.

Sweep can help
Sweep makes sustainability work for your business. Not the other way round. We connect all your sustainability data and turn it into business intelligence to help you unlock performance – from compliance and risk reduction, all the way to cost-savings, and market differentiation.
With Sweep, you can:
Lower costs through real-time tracking and insights
Strengthen supply chains with end-to-end visibility and engagement
Deliver audit-ready sustainability and climate reporting with confidence
Make sustainability intelligence available to everyone to optimize the business
