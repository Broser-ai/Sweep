As part of Climate Week NYC 2025, you’ll be able to find Sweep’s team of experts at Sustainability Live, organised by Sustainability Magazine. Catch the Sweep team at the following sessions.
Sweep workshop: Product carbon footprint: from data gaps to competitive advantage
Time:
1pm – 2pm EDT
Invite only.
Join carbon and sustainability experts for a practical session on how to tackle real-world challenges of product carbon footprinting, from missing data to supplier hurdles – and turning them into clear, actionable insights.
We’ll walk through what makes PCFs useful (or not.) How to overcome common roadblocks, and how technology helps to manage complex and changing data to stay aligned with business strategy, and build a footprinting process that actually supports R&D, sourcing, and product decisions.
Whether you’re just starting out or scaling across a complex product portfolio, you’ll leave with strategies to make PCFs faster, more accurate, and more valuable to your business.
Panel: Sustainable Procurement & Supply Chain
Time:
14:20 – 3pm
Location:
Stage 2
This panel examines how procurement leaders are embedding ESG into supplier engagement, leveraging data for scope 3 emissions reductions, and fostering resilient, transparent value chains. Learn how forward-thinking organisations are transforming procurement from a cost centre into a catalyst for sustainable growth and competitive advantage.
