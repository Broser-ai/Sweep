AGENDA
Please note the event will be in German
15:30 – Check-in & welcome coffee
16:00 – Introduction and update from Sweep and Swisscom
16:15 – Customer story
16:40 – Managing ESG data for multi-framework compliance: CSRD, GRI, and more
As regulatory requirements evolve, managing ESG data across multiple frameworks (CSRD, GRI, CDP) becomes increasingly complex. Explore best practices for centralized data management, automation, and ensuring audit-ready compliance, illustrated through Swisscom’s approach to streamlined ESG reporting.
16:55 – A Conversation Between Swiss Founders on Unlocking Value
Two Swiss founders – Raphael Gueller (Sweep) and Sebastian Glink (Climada.Tech) come together for an open conversation on climate risk and value creation. Drawing on their firsthand experience building Sweep and Climada, they explore how climate risk intelligence and sustainability data intersect, share practical lessons from the field, and discuss how organizations can turn climate complexity into informed decisions and strategic advantage during the climate transition.
17:05 – Building accurate Scope 3 emissions
Spend-based calculations provide a starting point, but they lack the precision needed for credible Scope 3 reporting. Learn how to transition to product-level emissions data, engage your suppliers effectively, and build a more accurate carbon footprint.
