Last updated
May 06, 2026
The IDC MarketScape, a widely used vendor assessment model, evaluated 17carbon management platforms.
The MarketScape highlights “a key element of Sweep’s vision is the integration of AI to enhance data quality, enable predictive analytics, and provide valuable insights.” Its architecture supports high data accuracy, real-time validation, and consistent reporting across the business.
Sweep is proud to be recognised as a Leader in the IDC MarketScape with leading scores on both product capabilities and strategies. This reflects our focus on building a platform that helps organisations manage carbon data with precision and take meaningful action.
Rachel Delacour
CEO and Co-Founder
Key strengths cited in the report
Focus on skills-based AI:
“The platform’s architecture supports skill-based AI, prioritizing fewer, highly capable agents that draw from diverse emission factor databases and proprietary intelligence.”
Enterprise-grade data management core:
“Leveraging AI-driven automation to support a “load once use everywhere” the Sweep platform efficiently manages data mapping processes, promoting both precision and adherence to reporting requirements.”
Mapping complex organizational hierarchies:
“Sweep enables organizations to map intricate hierarchical structures within its platform, supporting multi-entity, multisite, and multi-subsidiary configurations.”
Enabling faster LCA creation:
“The platform integrates emission factors from recognized databases, significantly reducing manual effort and data handling complexity. As a result, the customer cut the time required to run LCAs by half compared with prior approaches and achieved measurable cost savings by reducing reliance on external consultants for carbon footprint analysis.”
A platform built for sophisticated carbon management
Sweep’s AI-powered software platform enables businesses to turn ESG and carbon data into measurable business advantages. From emissions mapping to building transition plans and automating regulatory reporting, Sweep empowers businesses to act on sustainability with speed, precision, and transparency – regardless of sector or how complex their operations may be.
