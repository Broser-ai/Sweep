Sweep named a sustainability management software leader
According to “IDC MarketScape Worldwide Carbon Accounting and Management Applications 2024 Vendor Assessment” Report
About the IDC MarketScape Report
IDC, the US-headquartered global market intelligence firm which counts more than 5,000 employees and 1,300 analysts worldwide, reviewed more than 100 vendors and shortlisted 21.
The report notes that while Sweep is suitable for organizations of all sizes due to its graduated model,
“the platform is particularly well suited for large organizations as well as organizations with complex value chains (retail, manufacturing, and finance)”
.
Sweep has been recognized for:
“Proven capabilities to support millions of data that can be dynamically manipulated and leveraged for multidimensional analysis”
“Ensuring data integrity by validating information in real time and tracking inconsistencies”
“Enabling customers to largely automate data ingestion, with roughly 90% of data uploads automated”
“Serving as a decision-making tool for sustainability and finance managers to calculate ROI, estimate payback periods, and select the most efficient reduction initiatives”
“Users can quickly get an understanding of supplier emissions based on CDP estimates or gather more granular data through customized surveys”
“The tool mainly adapts to us, not us having to format everything to fit into the tool”
