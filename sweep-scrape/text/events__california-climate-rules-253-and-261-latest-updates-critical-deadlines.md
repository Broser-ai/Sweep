## Content


EVENT
California SB 253 & SB 261: latest updates and critical deadlines
Check out this essential webinar with Kate Gordon, former Senior Climate Policy Advisor to Governor Gavin Newsom, for insider insight on the upcoming changes and deadlines to California's climate rules.
Date
Nov 13, 2025
Language
English
Category
On demand
Last updated
November 14, 2025
California’s SB 253 and SB 261 will require thousands of companies to report greenhouse gas emissions and climate-related financial risks starting in 2026. This webinar gives you the insider knowledge you need to prepare.
Featured speakers:
Kate Gordon, CEO, CA FWD: and
former Senior Climate Policy Advisor to Governor Gavin Newsom
, where Kate helped to develop the California Laws and led the California’s interagency “climate cabinet”
Rachel Delacour, CEO & Cofounder, Sweep
Lizzy Selvik, Senior Platform & Sustainability Expert, Sweep
What you’ll learn:
Clear guidance on compliance requirements
– who’s in scope, key deadlines, and what you need to report under both laws
Insider perspectives on regulatory intent
– Kate’s firsthand insights on what regulators really want to see
Practical steps for your organization
– how to assess your readiness, streamline data collection, and prepare for third-party assurance Strategic business opportunities – how to turn compliance into competitive advantage
