The French Association of Institutional Investors (Af2i) is organizing, in partnership with Option Finance and GreenUnivers, the second edition of the Time to Change forum, which will bring together all the actors of sustainable finance and energy transition.
We are pleased to inform you that we will be sponsoring this event, with two presentations on climate change and green finance.
Our Chief Impact Officer, Julien Dernomandie, will speak on March 29 at 5:45 pm, during the conference: How to create a "Net Zero" industry?
The following day, March 29, at 12:25 pm, our VP of Climate Finance, Marie-Anne Vincent, will speak at the conference: ESG, a strategic priority and value creation lever for private equity managers.
