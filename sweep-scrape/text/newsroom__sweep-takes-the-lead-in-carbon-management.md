Sweep takes the lead in carbon management
Last updated
September 24, 2024
Press release
Recognized in the
Verdantix Green Quadrant: Enterprise Carbon Management Software 2023 benchmark report
as trusted sustainability partner
Paris, 30 November 2023
– Sweep, a leading software platform specializing in carbon and ESG data management, has been recognized as one of the leading performers in the recently released Green Quadrant: Enterprise Carbon Management Software 2023 report.
The comprehensive report, which evaluated 19 prominent enterprise carbon management software vendors, highlighted Sweep’s exceptional capabilities in carbon management.  The report also underlined its commitment to addressing the evolving landscape of environmental, social, and governance (ESG) initiatives.
“At Sweep, we believe in the power of data and the strength of our software platform – we’re architects of sustainability. Our partnerships with industry leaders underscore our commitment to providing not just a platform but a comprehensive solution, tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients in their journey towards decarbonization.”
Rachel Delacour, CEO and co-founder of Sweep.
The Green Quadrant report, which uses the proprietary Verdantix methodology, including live briefings, desktop research, and vendor responses to a rigorous 70-point questionnaire covering 12 capability and 6 market momentum categories.
Sweep emerged as a leading player in enterprise carbon management software. The SaaS provider excelled in its data acquisition, carbon emissions calculation, and net zero program management capabilities essential to companies’ decarbonization programs and regulatory commitments on emissions reporting.
“The growth of carbon management software is being driven by companies’ voluntary and regulatory commitments to carbon emissions reporting and decarbonization. This is driving demand for sophisticated software solutions that not only measure emissions, but also forecast, monitor decarbonization initiatives and support investment strategies.” – Adam Barnard, Principal Analyst at Verdantix
“Precision is key in carbon management. The Sweep data platform is committed to granular data analytics, auditability and transparency. Every feature is geared towards reducing the complexity for our customers as they track and act on their carbon data to build a sustainable business”
Yannick Chaze, CTO and co-founder of Sweep
Working against regulatory pressures, stakeholder expectations, and technological advancements, Sweep has positioned itself as a key player, establishing alliances with leading service provider partners such as ERM, 3Degrees, AXA Climate, BearingPoint, Capgemini, Malk Partners and EcoAct.
Sweep’s latest developments which involve integrating AI-driven insights and predictive analytics highlight the company’s commitment to innovation and staying at the forefront of the evolving carbon management frontier.
For organizations navigating the complexities of decarbonization, such as current customers Banque de France, HP, and L’Oréal, Sweep has solidified its position as a leading software platform for building a sustainable and resilient future with data.
