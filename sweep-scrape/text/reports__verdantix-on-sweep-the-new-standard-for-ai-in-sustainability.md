Verdantix on Sweep: The new standard for AI in sustainability
Verdantix shows how Sweep’s AI agents streamline carbon and ESG data, automate complex tasks, and deliver trustworthy, enterprise ready sustainability workflows.
Category
Reports
Last updated
December 04, 2025
You’re under pressure to deliver reliable carbon and ESG data while meeting tighter regulations and rising expectations across your business. This new Verdantix report shows how Sweep’s AI agents give leaders a meaningful advantage, turning complex sustainability tasks into simple, conversation driven workflows that free up time and unlock better decisions.
Verdantix finds that Sweep’s agentic AI is already transforming the way teams work by helping them move from manual effort to intelligent automation. The report highlights how these agents enable you to:
Map and clean raw sustainability data
in moments
Select accurate emissions factors
at scale
Draft disclosure ready content
from your existing data
Build workflows and visualizations
simply by describing what you need
What stands out most in the Verdantix assessment is Sweep’s commitment to responsible, enterprise ready AI. With strong governance, data protection, model transparency and impact tracking, Sweep delivers AI you can trust.
