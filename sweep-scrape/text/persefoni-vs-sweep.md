Complete ESG integration
Sweep unifies carbon accounting with full ESG data management, tracking carbon alongside social and governance metrics in one platform. Support CSRD’s full scope with the same data quality and audit trails across all dimensions.
Persefoni focuses on carbon accounting, especially financed emissions, and does not support broader ESG management
Regulatory coverage
Sweep supports complete CSRD compliance (all ESRS topics including E, S, and G), alongside ISSB, GRI, CDP, SB 253/261, TCFD, and SFDR. All from a single trusted dataset with audit-ready outputs. Multi-framework support means one implementation serves every current and future reporting need.
Persefoni delivers robust climate-aligned reporting for GHG Protocol, PCAF, ESRS E1, and TCFD, primarily focused on climate disclosures for financial institutions.
Collaboration at scale
Sweep enables end-to-end supplier engagement with real-time surveys, shared dashboards, and automated data collection to manage accountability across your entire value chain. Suppliers can track their own footprints and contribute directly to reduction plans.
Persefoni offers strong carbon data integrations, but its supplier collaboration capabilities are still under development.
Flexible access and scalable deployment
Sweep offers modular pricing and platform configuration that scales with organizational complexity, with guided learning via Sweep School to build internal capability without external consultants. Large teams can collaborate seamlessly with role-based access and enterprise-grade workflows.
Persefoni targets large enterprises and financial institutions. Pricing increases significantly for full platform functionality beyond basic footprinting.
AI-powered Scalability

Sweepy provides domain expertise with citations, not just generic answers.
Persefoni applies AI primarily within emissions calculations and PCAF methodologies, offering precision for carbon accounting but less automation across enterprise ESG workflows.

FAQs: Persefoni vs Sweep
How does Sweep differ from Persefoni’s carbon accounting software?
Sweep is a full sustainability management platform that combines carbon accounting, ESG data, supply-chain transparency, and regulatory compliance in one system, making it one of the strongest Persefoni alternatives for any company that needs more than carbon-only reporting.
Both Sweep and Persefoni are often considered contenders for the best carbon accounting software solutions, with each tool offering robust features for organizations seeking comprehensive sustainability management that addresses environmental impact and carbon footprint.
While Persefoni software excels at financed emissions and PCAF-aligned calculations, Sweep expands beyond climate, with climate change as a driving force for these solutions, to include social and governance metrics, integrated workflows across various departments, allowing teams to collaborate seamlessly within the platform, and real time insights that support broader sustainability goals and business growth.
Sweep’s user-friendly interface ensures that users can easily navigate the platform, while its key features, such as automated data collection, customizable dashboards, and advanced reporting, make it a powerful choice for companies with complex regulatory and sustainability requirements.
Which platform is better for managing complex regulatory requirements?
Sweep helps organisations ensure compliance with a wide range of regulations, including CSRD (all ESRS), SB 253/SB 261, CDP, GRI, ISSB, and global regulatory standards, as well as global standards for emissions measurement and reporting, with AI tools that automate mapping, disclosure creation, and audit-ready outputs, including the generation of standardized reports for compliance.
Persefoni focuses primarily on climate-centric frameworks such as GHG Protocol (a leading greenhouse gas reporting framework), PCAF, and TCFD, making it strong for carbon-first teams but less comprehensive for companies facing multi-topic sustainability reporting across ESG and industry-specific regulations, while also enabling users to report emissions data for regulatory compliance.
How do Persefoni and Sweep compare on data collection and emissions tracking?
Sweep automates data collection from various sources, unifies emissions data, energy consumption, and esg data, including sustainability data, and provides real time analytics that allow teams to effectively track progress and identify opportunities to reduce carbon emissions across the entire value chain.
Persefoni offers strong tools to calculate emissions, particularly Scope 1 and financed emissions, but relies more on manual effort for broader ESG datasets, making Sweep better suited for companies handling large data volumes, complex supply chains, or multi-entity structures needing continuous emissions tracking.
Many organizations rely on these platforms for comprehensive emissions tracking and sustainability management.
Which solution is better for driving action beyond carbon accounting?
Sweep transforms sustainability insights into action with advanced analytics features, decarbonisation planning, key metrics dashboards, and collaboration tools that engage relevant stakeholders across the organisation, enabling companies to improve energy efficiency, enhance risk management, and strengthen overall ESG performance.
Persefoni is well suited for teams focused primarily on carbon calculations and financed emissions, but Sweep’s broader carbon management and ESG functionality makes it a more powerful Persefoni competitor for smaller businesses, mid market companies, and enterprises seeking end-to-end emissions management and safety management capabilities.
