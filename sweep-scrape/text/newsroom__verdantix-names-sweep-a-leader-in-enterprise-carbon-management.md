Category

Last updated

News announcement
Carbon Management
S
weep, the sustainability data management platform, has been named a Leader in the 2026 Verdantix Green Quadrant for Enterprise Carbon Management by Verdantix, an independent research and advisory firm.
This latest recognition underlines Sweep’s commitment to empowering organizations worldwide to manage their carbon data at scale and take real value-building action on decarbonization.
Verdantix recognized Sweep for its leading capabilities across four areas critical to effective emissions management:
Value chain emissions management
Organizational structure
Carbon financial management
Data management
Sweep achieved the highest score across the entire field of 21 vendors in value chain emissions management. The platform also earned leading scores in data management and for its ability to adapt to customers’ business structures.
These are consistently among Sweep’s strongest areas, and key differentiators: the ability to handle complex, multi-source emissions data at the scale of your entire value chain, in ways competitors can’t match.
Verdantix identified Sweep as particularly well-suited for organizations that need to navigate complex supplier networks and manage growing sustainability workloads.
The platform stood out for its ability to:
Support sustainability teams managing heavy workloads
Collect data for firms with limited resources but complex supplier networks
Make supplier onboarding easier with a user-friendly interface
Provide AI assistance for all users, directly within the platform
Offer deep configurability across organizations, dashboards and workflows
Use AI to assist with data mapping, validation and transformation
“Being named a Leader in the Verdantix Green Quadrant is a reflection of what our customers are achieving every day with Sweep,”
said Rachel Delacour, CEO and Co-founder of Sweep.
“The recognition of our value chain management, data capabilities and configurability validates exactly what we’ve set out to build: a software platform that can handle the real complexity of sustainability data, at scale, across entire organizations and supply chains. Our goal is to give every business the tools they need to move from data collection to value- and resilience-building action.”
With its focus on actionable sustainability intelligence, cross-functional collaboration and value chain management, Sweep continues to set the standard for carbon management and sustainability data solutions. As organizations face growing pressure to deliver on their environmental commitments, Sweep is ready to help them turn sustainability from a reporting exercise into a driver of lasting business value.

About Verdantix
Verdantix is an independent research and advisory firm that serves a global client base consisting of the world’s most innovative corporations, technology and services vendors, and investors. Our insights and analysis form a foundation of the most granular data available in the marketplaces we serve. This allows us to make highly accurate far-reaching forecasts and big-picture predictions that business leaders depend on when they are setting out to reach their most important goals.
