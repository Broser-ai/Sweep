How To Accelerate Your Sustainability Transformation in 2024 and Beyond
During this Sweep webinar, our CEO, Rachel Delacour will be joined by guest speakers, Thomas Husson, Vice President and Principal Analyst at Forrester and Andrew Winston, co-author of ‘Net Positive: How Courageous Companies Thrive by Giving More than They Take,’ to share a multi-stakeholder perspective on the ways you can accelerate your company’s sustainability transformation.

The increasing importance of ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) issues has changed the way businesses operate across industries and regions. However, the ESG landscape is ever evolving and there are new trends coming into force that will shape businesses in 2024 and beyond.
During this Sweep webinar, our CEO,
Rachel Delacour
will be joined by guest speakers,
Thomas Husson
,  Vice president and Principal Analyst at Forrester and
Andrew Winston
, co-author of ‘Net Positive: How Courageous Companies Thrive by Giving More than They Take,’ to share a multi-stakeholder perspective on the ways you can accelerate your company’s sustainability transformation.
Our expert panel will discuss:
The key sustainability and ESG trends for 2024 and beyond
How technology (including AI) can help reduce your environmental footprint
Company ESG maturity: Are you a leader or a laggard in sustainability transformation?
We will conclude the session with a short Q&A from our participants.
