Legal Notice
The privacy of your data is a big deal to us. In this policy, we will explain which data we collect and why; how your data is handled; and your rights to your data. We will never sell your data. This policy applies to all products and services built and maintained by our company, Sweep SAS.
1. Scope
This Privacy Policy covers Sweep SAS and its affiliates and is effective for all sites, portals or properties owned, controlled or managed by Sweep. This Policy is applicable to sweep.net and any other websites, and applications owned and operated by Sweep SAS, registered with the Companies Register of Montpellier under number 883 967 879, with its registered office at 80 Rue Isabelle Eberhardt, 34000 Montpellier, France. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the websites of our customers or Business Partners or to the websites of any other third parties, even if we have links to their websites on our website. This Policy outlines Sweep’s specific practices with regard to the collection, use, processing, transfer and/or storage of Personal Data (as defined below) in connection with User’s use of the Site in accordance with the Terms of Use. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, “Business Partners” means subcontractors, vendors, distributors, resellers and other entities with whom Sweep has ongoing business relationships to provide products, services and information to its customers. “Affiliates” means legal entities that are owned by or under direct common ownership with Sweep, “Personal Data” means information that can directly or indirectly identify you as an individual, such as your name, telephone number, address, billing information and email address or other similar information. Sweep may seek User’s explicit consent to process Personal Data collected by Sweep or voluntarily provided by User. In case, User does not grant the requested consent for the processing of its Personal Data or if User later informs Sweep by email at
privacy@sweep.net
of the withdrawal of its consent to the processing of its Personal Data, any further use of certain offerings or services offered by Sweep on the Site may be either prevented or limited in scope.

Sweep may use third-party vendors for online marketing and cookie collection. Unless stated otherwise in this Privacy Policy, Sweep processes Personal Data in the role of a Controller.
5. Sharing User’s Information
Sweep may share Personal Data with our affiliated and subsidiary companies or Business Partners for the execution of the services, to make Sweep’s business more responsive to User’s needs, to provide User with information about Sweep’s products and services, or for research and analysis. Sweep may transmit Personal Data to third-party vendors and hosting partners that provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and other technology and maintenance services required to operate and maintain this Site and any related services. Transfers to any third parties are covered under the provisions of this Policy. Sweep may also share Personal Data with Business Partners that help Sweep customise, analyze and/or improve its communications or business relationship with Users. These communications may include requests relating to Sweep’s products or services, including the Sweep Carbon management tool. In any of these instances where Sweep is sharing Users’ Personal Data, Sweep shall make sure that those third parties are committed to protecting Personal Data and have agreed in writing to provide such protection both in compliance with legal requirements and commensurate with the protections provided by Sweep. Sweep shall not disclose Personal Data to third parties for their own marketing purposes in absence of Users’ prior consent. Except as described in this Policy, Sweep shall not give, sell, rent, share or loan any personal information to any third party other than as outlined in this Policy. Legal reasons

(iii)

Sweep’s products, services and other web properties are operated in Europe. Any information provided to Sweep will be transferred to and stored in Europe.

Sweep has a robust IT Security strategy which includes policies, processes and controls designed to ensure the protection of any Personal Data, including a variety of security measures intended to prevent unauthorized access to Personal Data. Sweep’s IT Security strategy is available on the Site. Sweep evaluates and responds to reports of incidents that might involve unauthorized access to Personal Data. If Sweep becomes aware that Personal Data has been compromised or mishandled, Sweep shall report any such action or activity to the User in accordance with the GDPR.

Users may choose to receive or not receive marketing communications from Sweep by indicating its preferences on this Site. If the User would prefer not to receive future marketing communications or wish to change or modify information previously provided or delete inaccuracies, please contact us at
privacy@sweep.net.
Sweep shall respond to all requests within 30 days. Sweep shall retain User’s Personal Data on User’s account for as long as the account is active or as needed to provide you services and as necessary to comply with its legal, regulatory or compliance obligations. Even if User opts out of receiving marketing communications, Sweep may still communicate with User in connection with servicing any existing account User may have, fulfilling a request from User, or administering any promotion or any program in which User may have elected to participate. Some marketing sent to the User, including email marketing, may also be personalized based on the User’s visits to this Site, its browsing and/ or purchase history with Sweep. In addition, when the User clicks on some links in email marketing, a cookie may have been placed on the User’s browser. This cookie would be linked to the User’s email address and used to gather information about the products and services viewed on this Site. The information gathered may be used to personalize and customize future email marketing messages to send to the User. Users may opt out of this use by clicking on the unsubscribe link provided in every personalized email marketing message sent by Sweep.

13. Transfer of Personal Data
Personal Data collected may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which Sweep’s affiliates and subsidiary companies or Business Partners maintain facilities. Sweep provides appropriate levels of protection to safeguard any Personal Data including providing adequate protection for any transfer of Personal Data to a country outside the EU/EEA. These safeguards include data protection agreements, incorporating the new EU standard contractual clauses (standard contractual clauses between controllers and processors under Article 28(7), Article 6(1)(a) and Article 6(1)(f) of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council and Article 29(7) of Regulation (EU) 2018/1725 of the European Parliament and of the Council).
14. User’s Rights
If the User would like to exercise any of its rights under the GDPR or any applicable data protection laws or receive more information about its rights, the User shall contact Sweep via email at
privacy@sweep.net
or by post at 80 Rue Isabelle Eberhardt, 34000 Montpellier. Sweep shall promptly respond to all requests from individuals seeking to exercise their rights described in this Privacy Policy and pursuant to applicable data protection laws. Some of the following rights may not be applicable depending on the situation:
Right of access
: The User has the right to gain access to information about the personal data that Sweep processes about the User.
Right to rectification
: The User has the right to get their personal data updated or corrected. Upon User’s request, Sweep shall promptly correct its information inaccurately stored and/or supplement incomplete personal data completed by including a supplementary statement provided by User.
Right to erasure/right to be forgotten
: The User has the right to request to permanently delete its personal information. The User can make such a request if the User believes that the personal data provided are no longer necessary in relation to the purpose for which the personal data were collected or otherwise processed.
Right to restrict the processing activities
: The User has the right to restrict Sweep’s processing activities. If the User chooses to restrict Sweep’s processing activities regarding certain personal data, the User may not be able to use the Site properly.
Right to data portability
: The User has the right to transfer their Personal Data, when possible.
Right to object
: The User has the right to object to the processing of its Personal Data that is carried out on the basis of legitimate interests, such as direct marketing.
Right Not to be Subject to Automated Decision-Making
: The User has the right not to be subject to automated decision-making, including profiling, which produces legal effects. Sweep and its affiliates do not currently engage in the foregoing on its Site or products and services. If the User is not satisfied with the way Sweep treats its Personal Data, the User shall reach out to Sweep at all times to discuss the issue. However, the User always has the right to lodge a complaint to a supervisory authority (CNIL in France).
15. Retention Periods
Sweep retains the User’s Personal Data as long as it is necessary for the purposes stated above, if not stated otherwise in this Privacy Policy. Sweep might process the User’s Personal Data longer than stated above if and when Sweep determines it is legally necessary to do so.
16. Effective Date and Notification of Changes to the Privacy policy
This Privacy Policy is effective as of October 2022 and is reviewed periodically. Sweep reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. If Sweep materially changes this Privacy Policy, Sweep shall post a prominent notice on this Site. Changes are effective as of the date of the post on the Privacy Policy page of this Site.
