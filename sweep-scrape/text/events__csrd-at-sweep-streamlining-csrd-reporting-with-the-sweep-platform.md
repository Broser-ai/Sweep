# CSRD at Sweep: Streamlining CSRD reporting with the Sweep Platform - Sweep


**Description:** Sweep is the sustainability intelligence platform that turns sustainability data into measurable business performance.


## Content


EVENT
CSRD at Sweep: Streamlining CSRD reporting with the Sweep Platform
Join us on March 28, 2024 to deep-dive into Sweep’s powerful software platform, taking a close look at our CSRD reporting module.
Date
Mar 28, 2024
Time
3:00 PM
Language
English
Category
Webinar
Last updated
November 04, 2024
CSRD
This webinar is dedicated to our Partners.  We will focus on our latest platform developments responding to the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) and how Sweep will help. By the end of this webinar, you will know more about our CSRD module and how it will improve the quality, audibility and traceability of ESG data.Learn about:
Sweep’s CSRD vision and our product is meeting our customer’s needs;
The latest update on Sweep’s CSRD module development;
A product deep-dive with key insights and live demo.
We will reserve time for an in-depth Q&A to tackle your questions.
Speakers:
